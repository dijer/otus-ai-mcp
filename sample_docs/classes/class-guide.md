# Path of Exile 2: Class Guide — All Six Base Classes

## Overview

Path of Exile 2 features six base classes, each tied to a primary attribute combination. At the end of Act 2, each class selects an Ascendancy specialization that defines the endgame build direction.

---

## 1. Warrior (Strength)

### Playstyle
- Melee-focused frontline fighter.
- Relies on Armor mitigation and high life pool.
- Favors slow heavy attacks with large stagger values.

### Signature Skills
- Boneshatter: slam that stacks self-damage charges but deals massive area damage.
- Stampede: mobility charge with knockback.
- Warlord's Mark: curse that generates life gain on hit.

### Ascendancy Options
- Titan: increases stagger output and armor values; best for tanking.
- Berserker: increases rage stacks and damage; higher risk, higher reward.

### Optimal Boss-Killing Approach
- Time heavy slams into stagger windows.
- Use Granite Flask for physical-heavy encounters.
- Sacrifice some damage uptime for survivability during Phase 2 transitions.

### Strengths
- Highest base life pool.
- Most effective at triggering boss staggers.
- Best physical mitigation of all classes.

### Weaknesses
- Slowest movement speed base.
- Vulnerable to elemental damage without heavy investment.
- Long skill animations make reactive dodging harder.

---

## 2. Ranger (Dexterity)

### Playstyle
- Ranged mobile attacker.
- High evasion, low life pool.
- Fast attack speed, moderate single-hit damage.

### Signature Skills
- Barrage: multi-shot burst with high single-target DPS.
- Rain of Arrows: AoE ground coverage for add-heavy encounters.
- Dash: movement skill with additional evasion frames.

### Ascendancy Options
- Deadeye: increases projectile speed and hit probability; best for ranged.
- Pathfinder: enhances flasks; provides flask-based survivability.

### Optimal Boss-Killing Approach
- Maintain maximum range to read telegraph windows earlier.
- Use Rain of Arrows for add management in simultaneous encounters.
- Rotate around boss to maintain optimal angle on boss weak points.

### Strengths
- Highest mobility of base classes.
- Longest attack range.
- Best evasion-based defense.

### Weaknesses
- Lowest life pool.
- No inherent status effect application.
- Struggles against enemies with high projectile immunity.

---

## 3. Witch (Intelligence)

### Playstyle
- Spell caster with summoning capability.
- Relies on Energy Shield as primary defense.
- High burst damage with longer cooldown cycles.

### Signature Skills
- Firestorm: AoE fire shower with sustained damage over time.
- Summon Skeleton Mages: persistent minion damage dealers.
- Arcane Surge: buff that increases spell damage after spending mana.

### Ascendancy Options
- Necromancer: enhances minion count and minion damage.
- Occultist: boosts energy shield and curse effectiveness.

### Optimal Boss-Killing Approach
- Maintain energy shield above 50% before attempting burst.
- Use minions to body-block boss movement in arena-denial encounters.
- Cycle through cooldown spells methodically; do not burn all cooldowns at once.

### Strengths
- Highest burst spell damage.
- Can delegate damage to minions.
- Best energy shield pool.

### Weaknesses
- No dodge roll distance bonus.
- Chaos damage bypasses energy shield by default.
- Long cast animations punish mid-cast interruptions.

---

## 4. Duelist (Strength + Dexterity)

### Playstyle
- Hybrid physical melee fighter with high crit chance.
- Moderate life and evasion combination.
- Focuses on critical strike chains and bleed application.

### Signature Skills
- Reave: horizontal sweeping strike with stacking range bonus.
- Whirling Blades: mobility attack skill.
- Double Strike: two rapid consecutive hits with high crit multiplier.

### Ascendancy Options
- Gladiator: enhances bleed and shield-related mechanics.
- Champion: provides aura-based party support and fortify buff.

### Optimal Boss-Killing Approach
- Stack bleeds during open punish windows.
- Use Whirling Blades for rapid repositioning between abilities.
- Maintain Fortify buff by landing consecutive attacks without interruption.

### Strengths
- Highest critical strike multiplier base.
- Flexible attack range (melee to mid-range).
- Strong bleed-based sustained damage.

### Weaknesses
- No reliable elemental damage without investment.
- Fortify buff drops during phases requiring ranged play.
- Bleed damage reduced when boss is stationary.

---

## 5. Shadow (Dexterity + Intelligence)

### Playstyle
- Trap and mine specialist with high poison application.
- Relies on setting up hazards before engaging boss directly.
- Lowest direct damage; highest sustained damage through poison.

### Signature Skills
- Poisonous Concoction: high-stack poison projectile.
- Trap Throw: places triggered trap at target location.
- Smoke Mine: creates blind zone and teleport anchor.

### Ascendancy Options
- Assassin: maximizes poison stacks and critical strike chance.
- Saboteur: enhances trap and mine mechanics.

### Optimal Boss-Killing Approach
- Pre-place traps before phase transitions.
- Use Smoke Mine for fast repositioning during burst windows.
- Sustain 15+ poison stacks consistently through Poisonous Concoction spam.

### Strengths
- Highest sustained chaos damage.
- Traps apply damage passively without player risk.
- Best at exploiting standing bosses during stagger locks.

### Weaknesses
- Trap setup time is wasted if boss is mobile.
- Low base HP requires exceptional positioning.
- Poison is ineffective against chaos-immune enemies.

---

## 6. Templar (Strength + Intelligence)

### Playstyle
- Hybrid caster-melee with aura specialization.
- Balances spell and attack damage.
- Best party support utility of all classes.

### Signature Skills
- Incinerate: channeled fire beam with ramp-up damage.
- Holy Fire: persistent ground burn aura.
- Shield Charge: charge attack with knockback.

### Ascendancy Options
- Inquisitor: enhances elemental damage and penetration.
- Guardian: maximizes aura effect and party survivability.

### Optimal Boss-Killing Approach
- Use Incinerate against stationary bosses during long punish windows.
- Shield Charge for rapid gap close after dodge roll.
- Holy Fire provides passive AoE damage on adds without player input.

### Strengths
- Best elemental penetration base.
- Strong in add-heavy encounters through passive auras.
- Moderate life and energy shield split survivability.

### Weaknesses
- Incinerate channel interrupted by movement or hits.
- Guardian ascendancy underperforms in solo encounters.
- Worst base evasion of all classes.
