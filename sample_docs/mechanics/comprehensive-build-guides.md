# Path of Exile 2: Comprehensive Build Guides and Economy

## Part 1: Life-Based Attack Builds

### 1.1 Earthquake Berserker (Standard Physical Scaling)

**Core Mechanics**: Earthquake detonates at exactly 1.6 seconds after impact, dealing area damage equal to 150% of initial hit damage. Stacking aftershocks is the primary damage multiplier.

**Passive Tree Path**:
- Start: pick up Cleaving (12% increased attack damage)
- Path to: Elasticity nodes (+20% armor, +15% life)
- Allocate: Physical Damage nodes (10 nodes = +150% physical)
- Keystone: (optional) Acrobatics for +30% dodge if you skip armor nodes

**Gear Priorities**:
1. **Main Hand**: 2H axe or sword with 300+ physical damage base
   - Recommended bases: Poleaxe (300 pDPS), Maul (280 pDPS)
   - Suffixes: +Cold resistance 30%, Adds 20 fire damage
2. **Body Armor**: Plate Armor with 300+ armor
   - Life rolls required: +100 flat minimum
   - Recommended: 6-socket for Earthquake + Melee Splash + Multistrike + Ruthless + Added Fire + Support
3. **Boots**: +30% movement speed minimum
   - Secondary: +80 armor per 2 seconds (if flinch protection not taken)
4. **Rings/Amulet**: Life scaling only
   - Each ring: +60-80 flat life target
   - Each amulet: +100 flat life target
5. **Flasks**: 2 life, 1 armor, 1 frenzy charge, 1 utility

**Stat Targets** (Act 4 Geonar kill):
- Life: 2,800+ 
- Physical damage: 500-700
- Armor: 4,000+
- Attack speed: 1.3-1.5

**DPS Calculation**: 
- Base Earthquake: 600 physical damage per hit
- Aftershock: 900 (150% of 600)
- With Multistrike: 3 hits per rotation (1.0 second) = 5,400 DPS base
- With +100% physical gear scaling: 10,800 DPS

**Rotation**: 
1. Position adjacent to boss
2. Cast Earthquake (1.0 second)
3. Wait for aftershock (1.6 seconds, but start next cast at 1.0s for overlap)
4. Repeat every 1.0 seconds for continuous aftershock overlap
5. If boss moves >10 units, reposition (0.5 seconds wasted)

**Scaling into Endgame**:
- Requires 3,500 life and 6,000 armor for tier 16 maps
- Double the physical scaling: aim for 1,000 physical damage
- Add elemental damage conversion: +300 fire damage for ignite scaling

### 1.2 Cyclone Warrior (Mobility + Survivability)

**Core Mechanics**: Cyclone is a movement skill that deals damage to all nearby enemies while moving. It scales with attack speed and movement speed multiplicatively.

`Cyclone_DPS = Base_Damage × Attack_Speed × (1 + Nearby_Enemies_Damage)`

With 2.0 attack speed and 3 nearby enemies (multiplier 1.3), Cyclone deals 2.6x base damage.

**Passive Tree Path**:
- Start: Martial Training (basic attack nodes)
- Path to: Bloodletting nodes (+20% attack speed, +15% attack damage)
- Allocate: Attack speed nodes (8 = +120% attack speed total)
- Keystone: Unbreakable (+800 poise, immunity to stagger) - mandatory for this build

**Gear Priorities**:
1. **Main Hand + Offhand**: Dual wield 1H swords or axes
   - Each hand: 200+ physical damage minimum
   - Combined DPS: 450+
2. **Body Armor**: Unique "Kaom's Heart" (500 flat life) or rare with +150 life
3. **Boots**: Kinetic Boots for +40% movement speed
4. **Rings**: 2x damage + resistances
5. **Flasks**: Bleed immunity, poison immunity, 2 life, 1 armor

**Stat Targets** (Act 5 Meridian Watcher kill):
- Life: 3,200+
- Physical damage: 600+ combined
- Attack speed: 2.0+ (near cap)
- Movement speed: 130%+
- Poise: >1,000 (via Unbreakable)

**DPS Calculation**:
- Base damage per hit: 400 (200+200 from duals)
- Attack speed: 2.0
- Enemies nearby: 2 (multiplier 1.2)
- DPS = 400 × 2.0 × 1.2 = 960 DPS sustained

**Rotation**:
1. Cyclone directly into enemies
2. Move while attacking (both happen simultaneously)
3. Hold Cyclone until enemies die or reposition
4. On phase transition: pop defensive flask, move to safe zone, resume Cyclone

**Scaling into Endgame**:
- Unbreakable keystone eliminates stagger vulnerability (replaces need for 6,000 armor)
- Stack attack speed over damage (1% attack speed ≈ 1.5% DPS vs 1% damage = 1% DPS)
- Leech becomes mandatory: 5%+ physical leech for self-healing during combat

## Part 2: Caster Builds and Spell Scaling

### 2.1 Incinerate Templar (Pure Fire Scaling)

**Core Mechanics**: Incinerate reaches stage 5 (350% damage) after 5 casts. Each stage grants +70% damage:
- Stage 1: 70% damage (0.5 seconds)
- Stage 2: 140% (1.0 second cumulative)
- Stage 3: 210% (1.5 seconds)
- Stage 4: 280% (2.0 seconds)
- Stage 5: 350% (2.5+ seconds, damage caps here)

Maintaining stage 5 uptime requires consistent casting. Dropping below 5 casts within 4 seconds reverts to stage 1.

**Passive Tree Path**:
- Start: Spell Power nodes (Pyromancy)
- Path to: Fire Mastery (+50% fire damage)
- Allocate: Cast speed nodes (10 = +150% cast speed total)
- Keystone: Spell Amplification (+25% spell damage) or Elemental Mastery (+20% elemental)

**Gear Priorities**:
1. **Weapon**: Wand + Shield or 2H staff
   - Wand: +80 fire damage minimum, +1 spell level preferred
   - Staff: 120+ fire damage, +2 spell level preferred
2. **Body Armor**: Astral Plate or similar (high ES base)
   - Life + ES rolls required: +80 life, +100 ES
3. **Boots**: Movement speed +25%
4. **Rings**: +fire damage, +cast speed
5. **Amulet**: +fire damage, +1 spell level
6. **Flasks**: Immune to ignite, immune to shock, 1 mana, 2 utility

**Stat Targets** (Act 3 Ashen Magistrate kill):
- Life: 2,500+
- Energy Shield: 1,000+
- Fire damage: 300-400 flat
- Cast speed: 2.0+
- Mana: 300+

**DPS Calculation**:
- Base Incinerate: 200 fire damage per stage
- Stage 5: 200 × 3.5 = 700 damage per cast
- Cast time: 0.5 seconds (base 0.7 with -2.5 cast speed)
- DPS = 700 / 0.5 = 1,400 DPS sustained
- With +100% fire damage gear: 2,800 DPS

**Rotation**:
1. Ensure stage 5 uptime: cast continuously every 0.5 seconds
2. Every 4 seconds: pause 0.5 seconds (prevents overflowing mana)
3. On phase transition: move to safe zone, resume after 1 second
4. Use instant mana potions if mana drops below 50

**Scaling into Endgame**:
- Requires 3,000 life + 1,500 ES for tier 16 maps
- Layer elemental ailments: ignite (burn) + shock (vulnerability) for 150% more damage
- Convert 30% physical damage to fire: scale both bases together

### 2.2 Spell Echo Witch (Multi-Projectile Build)

**Core Mechanics**: Spell Echo repeats linked spell once with exactly 60% less damage. The repeat is a separate cast for cooldown purposes.

Combining Spell Echo with multi-projectile spells (Ice Spear, Fireball) causes exponential damage:
- Base spell: 3 projectiles
- Spell Echo: 3 projectiles (60% damage) = 1.8 DPS
- Total: 4.8x base DPS after two casts

**Passive Tree Path**:
- Start: Arcanist nodes (spell damage)
- Path to: Multi-projectile nodes (+2 projectiles)
- Allocate: Spell damage + cast speed equally (balance is key)
- Keystone: Increased Spell Damage keystone (+50% spell damage, -20% cast speed)

**Gear Priorities**:
1. **Weapon**: Scepter with +1 spell level and +50 spell damage
2. **Offhand**: Shield with +2 spell level preferred
3. **Body Armor**: Rare with +100 life minimum
4. **Boots**: +35% movement speed
5. **Rings**: +spell damage, +spell level
6. **Flasks**: Poison immunity, bleed immunity, 1 mana, 2 utility

**Stat Targets** (Act 4 Draev kill):
- Life: 2,800+
- Spell damage: 400+ flat
- Cast speed: 1.8+
- Projectiles: 6+ total (4 base + 2 from tree)
- Mana: 350+

**DPS Calculation**:
- Base projectile: 250 spell damage
- 6 projectiles per cast: 1,500 damage
- Spell Echo repeat: 600 damage (60% less)
- Per cast-pair: 2,100 damage
- Cast time: 0.8 seconds (0.6 base + Spell Echo delay)
- DPS = 2,100 / 0.8 = 2,625 DPS sustained

**Rotation**:
1. Cast Spell Echo-linked spell every 0.8 seconds
2. Against single boss: aim all projectiles at center
3. Against adds: spread projectiles to hit multiple targets simultaneously

**Scaling into Endgame**:
- Requires conversion of 50% damage to chaos for poison scaling
- Layer poison stacks: each poison refreshes if damage >0.5 second
- Stack multi-projectile nodes and passives aggressively (goal: 10+ projectiles)

## Part 3: Hybrid and Unique Builds

### 3.1 Mind Over Matter Hierophant (Mana Scaling)

**Core Mechanic**: Mind Over Matter passive absorbs exactly 30% of incoming damage as mana cost instead of life. This requires maintaining 30% of your HP value in mana reserves at all times.

`Safe_Mana = 0.3 × Life`

If your life is 3,000, you need 900 mana reserved minimum.

**Passive Tree Path**:
- Start: Spell damage nodes (Magister line)
- Path to: Mana nodes (+100 mana per node, 10 nodes = +1,000 mana)
- Allocate: Mana regeneration nodes (+50% mana regen)
- Keystone: Mind Over Matter (+30% damage mitigation via mana)

**Gear Priorities**:
1. **Weapon**: 2H staff or wand + shield
   - Requirements: +1 spell level, high spell damage
   - Mana suffix: +150 mana minimum
2. **Body Armor**: Plate Armor with life + mana suffixes
   - Life: +100, Mana: +100
3. **Rings + Amulet**: +mana, +spell damage, +resistances
4. **Flasks**: 1 life, 1 mana (priority), 1 mana guard, 1 utility, 1 offensive

**Stat Targets** (Act 5 Void Architect kill):
- Life: 2,500+
- Mana: 750+ (30% of life as buffer)
- Mana regeneration: 100+/second
- Spell damage: 350+
- Cast speed: 2.0+

**DPS Calculation**:
- Spell damage per cast: 300 pure/elemental
- Cast time: 0.5 seconds
- DPS = 300 / 0.5 = 600 DPS
- Mana overhead: 900 mana / 100 regen = 9 seconds sustain before running dry
- Effective rotation: 4.5 seconds casting + 0.5 seconds mana pot = 5-second rotation

**Rotation**:
1. Cast primary spell repeatedly (every 0.5 seconds)
2. Every 5 seconds: consume mana potion (restores 400 mana, prevents running dry)
3. On phase transition: immediately pop mana pot, resume casting

**Scaling into Endgame**:
- Requires 1,200+ total mana for tier 16 maps (40% of 3,000 life)
- Stack mana regeneration: target 200+/second for comfortable sustain
- Layer spell gems: Vaal skills consume mana for massive damage bursts

## Part 4: Economy and Currency Value

### 4.1 Currency Hierarchy (Act 5+ Reference Prices)

Based on Chaos Orb baseline (= 1.0 value):

**Tier 1 (Endgame Currency)**:
- Exalted Orb: 150-180 chaos
- Divine Orb: 120-150 chaos
- Mirror of Kalandra: 200,000+ chaos (cosmetic use only)

**Tier 2 (High-Value Crafting)**:
- Annulment Orb: 60-80 chaos
- Chromatic Orb: 0.05 chaos (10 = 0.5 chaos)
- Alteration Orb: 0.02 chaos (50 = 1 chaos)

**Tier 3 (Common Currency)**:
- Chaos Orb: 1.0 (baseline)
- Regal Orb: 5-8 chaos
- Alchemy Orb: 0.3 chaos (3 = 1 chaos)
- Transmutation Orb: 0.04 chaos (25 = 1 chaos)

**Tier 4 (Scarce Materials)**:
- Fossil (rare type): 2-5 chaos
- Shard (Harbinger): 0.01 chaos (100 = 1 chaos)
- Oil (blue, uncommon): 0.5-1 chaos

### 4.2 Crafting Investment vs. Expected Value

Crafting a single item requires multiple orbs. Example:

**Budget Rare Armor (2,500 chaos investment)**:
1. Start with base item: 1 chaos
2. Apply 10 alchemies (3 chaos)
3. Reroll for life (5 chromatics = 0.25 chaos)
4. Add crafted mods (2 exalts = 300 chaos)
5. Final cost: ~305 chaos

**Expected value if sold**: 2,500-3,000 chaos (if perfect rolls)
**Break-even chance**: 12% (sell only 1/8 items at target price)

This creates a farming strategy: create 8 items per 2,500 chaos investment, expect to sell 1 at profit.

### 4.3 Map Sustain and Profit Margins

Tier 16 maps drop from tier 15 maps via sextant modifiers and scarab usage.

**Map farming rotation (per 10 maps)**:
1. Invest: 50 chaos in sextants and scarabs
2. Expected returns: 8 maps (80% drop rate) at 20 chaos average = 160 chaos
3. Net profit: 110 chaos per 10-map run

At 15 minutes per run, this yields 440 chaos/hour, or 0.37 exalted orbs/hour.

## Part 5: Ascendancy Specialization

### 5.1 Warrior Ascendancy: Berserker

**Passive Grant**: +50% attack damage, Frenzy charges on kill

**Unique mechanic**: Rage generation - dealing damage generates rage (1 per 5% overkill damage). Rage scales attack damage: each stack grants +1% attack damage.

Maximum rage: 50 stacks = +50% bonus damage. Losing combat resets rage over 2 seconds.

Builds using Rage focus on high attack speed to maintain rage uptime (goal: deal damage every 0.2 seconds to refresh rage constantly).

### 5.2 Ranger Ascendancy: Deadeye

**Passive Grant**: +2 projectiles (massive multiplier), +100% projectile accuracy

**Unique mechanic**: Avatar of the Hunt - grants movement speed per frenzy charge (10% per charge). With 3 frenzy charges = 130% movement speed.

This ascendancy enables kiting builds that others cannot match.

### 5.3 Witch Ascendancy: Templar Ascendant

**Passive Grant**: +1 spell level, +30% spell damage

**Unique mechanic**: Sacred Fire - spells deal physical + fire damage. Enables scaling both physical and elemental in single build.

This is the most flexible ascendancy for hybrid damage builds.

## Conclusion

These build guides provide templates for tackling Acts 2-6 and endgame content. Core principles:
- Balance defense and offense in a 60/40 ratio
- Scaling happens through tree pathing, not gear alone
- Economy knowledge directly translates to build power (buy better gear with profit)
- Ascendancy choice locks build archetype early; plan carefully
