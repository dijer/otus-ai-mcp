# Path of Exile 2: Advanced Crafting Strategies and Economy

## Part 1: Advanced Crafting Methods

### 1.1 Chaos Crafting

**Basic Chaos Craft**:
1. Start with blue item (2 mods)
2. Use chaos orb: reroll all mods
3. Cost: 1 chaos orb per roll
4. Goal: get 3 perfect mods, then use Regal

**Example Craft: Boots**:
- Start: blue boots with +50 life, +10 fire resistance
- Roll 1: chaos orb → +60 life, +20 cold res (bad)
- Roll 2: chaos orb → +70 life, +15 fire res, +20 armor (ok, 3 mods)
- Upgrade: Regal orb → +80 life, +20 fire res, +40 armor, +100 evasion (4th mod added!)
- Value added: ~50-100 chaos worth of stats

**Expected Cost-to-Benefit**:
- Investment: 20 chaos orbs (20 rolls average)
- Expected value: 1-2 exalted orbs worth (150-300 chaos)
- Profit: 100-200 chaos per successful craft (if lucky)

**Variance** (luck factor):
- Best case: perfect craft in 5 rolls (50 chaos profit)
- Worst case: 50 rolls, no perfect craft (-50 chaos loss)
- Average case: break even or slight profit (requires discipline)

### 1.2 Fossil Crafting

**Fossil Mechanics**:
- Fossils modify probability of mods appearing
- Each fossil shifts weights of different mod categories
- Multiple fossils: apply cumulative weight shifts

**Popular Fossil Combos**:

Combo 1: "Pure Physical"
- Fossils: Corroded (physical mods more common)
- Result: likely 2-3 physical damage mods
- Use case: physical weapon crafting
- Cost: 5 corroded fossils × 3 chaos = 15 chaos investment

Combo 2: "Pure Elemental"
- Fossils: Prismatic (elemental mods more common)
- Result: likely fire/cold/lightning mods
- Use case: elemental weapon crafting
- Cost: 5 prismatic × 5 chaos = 25 chaos investment

Combo 3: "Life Focused"
- Fossils: Auspicious (increases life mod frequency)
- Result: guaranteed life mod
- Use case: armor crafting
- Cost: 5 auspicious × 2 chaos = 10 chaos investment

**Fossil Crafting Success Rate**:
- Single fossil: 40% get desired mod
- Double fossils: 60% get desired mod
- Triple fossils: 75% get desired mod (expensive but reliable)

### 1.3 Essence Crafting

**Essence Mechanics**:
- Use essence on white item
- Essence applies guaranteed mod
- Other mods roll randomly

**Common Essences**:

Essence 1: "Essence of Torment" (physical damage):
- Guaranteed mod: +50-100 physical damage
- Cost: 5 chaos
- Use: weapon crafting
- Benefit: physical weapons almost always want this

Essence 2: "Essence of Wrath" (lightning damage):
- Guaranteed mod: +30-50 lightning damage
- Cost: 3 chaos
- Use: weapon crafting
- Benefit: enables lightning builds

Essence 3: "Essence of Greed" (life):
- Guaranteed mod: +100-150 life
- Cost: 4 chaos
- Use: body armor crafting
- Benefit: life is always valuable

**Essence Strategy**:
1. Buy white item base: 1 chaos
2. Use essence: 3-5 chaos
3. Regal orb upgrade: 5 chaos
4. Result: 3-4 mods, value 50-100 chaos
5. Profit if item sells: 30-80 chaos net

### 1.4 Alteration Spam (Budget Method)

**Alteration Orb Mechanics**:
- Cost: 0.02 chaos (very cheap)
- Effect: reroll blue item mods
- Goal: get 2 perfect mods on blue, then Regal

**Expected Results**:
- Alteration spam: 500 orbs average (500 × 0.02 = 10 chaos)
- Result: 2 perfect mods
- Regal cost: 5 chaos
- Total: 15 chaos investment
- Expected value: 50-100 chaos item
- Profit: 35-85 chaos (if sells)

**Volume Strategy**:
- Don't craft 1 item, craft 10 items simultaneously
- Expect 7 successes, 3 failures
- Net profit: 7 × 50 - 3 × 15 = 305 chaos profit (per batch)
- Time: 2 hours for batch
- Rate: 152 chaos/hour (good for casual players)

## Part 2: Flipping and Trading Strategy

### 2.1 Underpriced Item Hunting

**Strategy**:
1. Monitor market for 1 hour
2. Note typical price for item (e.g., Kaom's Heart at 200 chaos)
3. Look for outliers (someone listing at 150 chaos)
4. Buy immediately if >20% below average
5. Relist at average price

**Example Flip**:
- Normal price: 200 chaos
- Found: 150 chaos (25% below)
- Buy: 150 chaos
- Relist: 195 chaos (undercut competitors by 5)
- Sell: 195 chaos
- Profit: 45 chaos (30% ROI)

**Risks**:
- Item doesn't sell (tied up capital)
- Market drops overnight (loss on position)
- Listing expires (need to relist)
- Competition undercuts aggressively

### 2.2 Currency Flipping

**Exalted Orb Flipping** (league start):
- Day 1: Exalted at 60 chaos (many new players selling)
- Day 3: Exalted at 100 chaos (rarity sets in)
- Profit: buy 100 exalted on day 1 → sell 100 × (100-60) = 4,000 chaos profit

**Risk/Reward**:
- Capital needed: 6,000 chaos (100 × 60)
- Time: 3 days
- Profit: 4,000 chaos (67% ROI)
- Downside: price might not rise as expected

### 2.3 Arbitrage (Regional Price Differences)

**Concept**: Some regions have different prices (if multi-region)

**Example** (hypothetical):
- Region A: Exalted at 150 chaos
- Region B: Exalted at 140 chaos
- Buy in B: 100 orbs × 140 = 14,000 chaos
- Sell in A: 100 orbs × 150 = 15,000 chaos
- Profit: 1,000 chaos (7% ROI)
- Time: 2 hours (cross-region transfer)

**Current Status**: Path of Exile 2 is single-region (no arbitrage possible yet)

## Part 4: Market Dynamics and Pricing

### 4.1 Price Trends by League Phase

**Week 1 (Launch)**: Chaos Cheap, Exalted Expensive
- Exalted: 50-100 chaos (supply shortage)
- Chaos: abundant (new players earn quickly)
- Strategy: hoard exalted, flip when stable

**Week 2-4 (Early League)**: Economy Stabilizes
- Exalted: 100-150 chaos (supply increases)
- Chaos: moderate supply
- Strategy: farm maps for consistent income

**Week 5-12 (Mid League)**: Endgame Dominated
- Exalted: 150-200 chaos (peak value)
- Tier 16 maps: expensive (scarcity drives price)
- Strategy: invest in gear, farm endgame content

**Week 13+ (Late League)**: Diminishing Returns
- Exalted: 200-300 chaos (highest value, but dying league)
- Player exodus: prices become unstable
- Strategy: prepare for next league, sell off positions

### 4.2 Seasonal Effects

**League Start Bonus**:
- New players: inflated prices for common items
- Profit opportunity: if you have items, sell early
- Duration: first 2 weeks

**Mid-League Crunch**:
- Consolidation: wealthy players dominate
- Prices: stabilize, fewer opportunities
- Duration: weeks 3-8

**End-of-League Dump**:
- Players leaving: mass selling at any price
- Bargains: can buy endgame gear at 50% discount
- Duration: weeks 12-16

## Conclusion

Advanced crafting and trading requires:
1. **Knowledge**: understand each method's math
2. **Capital**: 100+ chaos to start flipping
3. **Patience**: don't force trades at bad prices
4. **Discipline**: know when to fold losing positions
5. **Timing**: buy when markets are emotional, sell when rational

Expert traders earn 1,000+ chaos/hour through combination of farming + flipping. Casual traders earn 100-300 chaos/hour through farming alone. The skill ceiling is high but achievable.
