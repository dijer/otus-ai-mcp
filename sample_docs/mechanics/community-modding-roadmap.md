# Path of Exile 2: Community, Modding, and Future Roadmap

## Part 1: Community Systems

### 1.1 Guild Systems

**Guild Functions**:
- Member limit: 50 players per guild
- Leadership: 1 leader + 5 officers
- Communication: private guild chat channel
- Benefits: shared guild stash (20 slots)

**Guild Activities**:
- Weekly wars (guild vs guild PvP tournaments)
- Cooperative farming (shared profit pools)
- Mentoring (veterans teach newcomers)
- Social events (seasonal gatherings)

**Guild Economy**:
- Guild tax: optional (0-10% of member earnings)
- Tier 1 guilds: 10,000+ members (social focus)
- Tier 2 guilds: 1,000-10,000 members (casual)
- Tier 3 guilds: 100-1,000 members (competitive)
- Tier 4 guilds: 10-100 members (hardcore)

### 1.2 Social Media Integration

**Streaming**:
- Twitch integration: built-in stream support
- Auto-notification: stream goes live → guild chat alerts
- Cosmetic rewards: viewers earn cosmetics for watching
- Tournament streaming: seasonal tournaments broadcast

**Reddit Community**:
- Subreddit: r/pathofexile2 (official partner community)
- Membership: 50,000+ subscribers
- Content: guides, memes, lore discussions
- Moderation: official moderators + community mods

**Discord Communities**:
- Official Discord: 100,000+ members
- Regional Discords: language-specific (20+ languages)
- Role-based channels: #hardcore, #build-guides, #trading
- Bot integration: price-check bot, wiki integration

### 1.3 Community Events

**Seasonal Races** (4-5 per league):
- Speed races (first to level 70)
- Challenge races (specific build restrictions)
- Hardcore races (permadeath racing)
- Prizes: 5,000-50,000 chaos equivalent

**Community Challenges**:
- "The Exile Challenge" (complete 40 challenges per season)
- "Hardcore Gauntlet" (survive 30 consecutive maps)

- Rewards: exclusive cosmetics, titles
