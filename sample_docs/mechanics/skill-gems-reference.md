# Path of Exile 2: Complete Skill Gem Reference

## Overview

Skill gems in Path of Exile 2 are items socketed into gear that provide active skills or support effects. There are three types: Active Skill Gems, Support Gems, and Aura Gems.

---

## Active Skill Gems by Damage Type

### Physical Damage Skills

#### Cleave
- Type: Attack, AoE, Melee
- Description: Strikes in a wide arc with both weapons simultaneously.
- Damage: 95% of weapon damage
- Attack Speed: 85% of base
- AoE radius: 1.8 meters
- Gem Quality Bonus: +0.5% AoE radius per quality level
- Support gem slots: 5
- Recommended supports: Melee Physical Damage, Fortify, Multistrike

#### Lacerate
- Type: Attack, Melee, Duration
- Description: Slashes enemies and inflicts bleed; if already bleeding, triggers an additional cut.
- Base Bleed chance: 35%
- Damage: 80% of weapon damage per hit (two hits)
- Gem Quality Bonus: +1% bleed chance per quality level
- Support gem slots: 5
- Recommended supports: Chance to Bleed, Brutality, Ruthless

#### Heavy Strike
- Type: Attack, Melee, Staggering
- Description: A single powerful blow that deals massive stagger buildup.
- Damage: 220% of weapon damage
- Stagger contribution: 3x normal
- Attack Speed: 60% of base
- Gem Quality Bonus: +2% damage per quality level
- Support gem slots: 5
- Recommended supports: Melee Physical Damage, Concentrated Effect, Added Fire Damage

#### Sunder
- Type: Attack, Melee, AoE, Duration
- Description: Strikes the ground, creating a wave of broken terrain that deals physical damage.
- Wave damage: 120% of weapon damage
- Wave duration: 1.2 seconds
- Gem Quality Bonus: +1% damage per quality level
- Support gem slots: 5
- Recommended supports: Melee Physical Damage, Faster Attacks, Fortify

---

### Fire Damage Skills

#### Fireball
- Type: Spell, Projectile, Fire, AoE
- Description: Launches a ball of fire that explodes on impact.
- Base Damage: 55-85 fire damage at gem level 1
- Explosion radius: 1.5 meters
- Mana cost: 9
- Cast time: 0.75 seconds
- Gem Quality Bonus: +1% chance to ignite per quality level
- Support gem slots: 5
- Recommended supports: Faster Casting, Spell Echo, Controlled Destruction

#### Flameblast
- Type: Spell, AoE, Fire, Channelled
- Description: Channels a growing ring of fire; releases after channel ends.
- Channel time: 0.8 to 3.0 seconds; more channel time increases damage and radius.
- Base Damage at max channel: 500-800 fire
- Mana cost: 4 per stage
- Gem Quality Bonus: +2% chance to ignite per quality level
- Support gem slots: 5
- Recommended supports: Elemental Focus, Concentrated Effect, Controlled Destruction

#### Incinerate
- Type: Spell, Fire, Channelled, AoE
- Description: Channels a beam of fire with increasing damage the longer it is held.
- DPS ramp: 100% at stage 1; 200% at stage 3; 350% at stage 5 (max stage)
- Mana cost: 3 per stage
- Gem Quality Bonus: +0.5% damage per quality level
- Support gem slots: 5
- Recommended supports: Faster Casting, Increased Area, Elemental Focus

#### Combustion
- Type: Support
- Description: Linked skills have 10% chance to ignite; ignited enemies take 15% increased fire damage.
- This support skill is especially effective in Incinerate builds.
- Mana cost multiplier: 130%

### Cold Damage Skills

#### Freezing Pulse
- Type: Spell, Projectile, Cold, Duration
- Description: Fires a pulse of cold energy that freezes enemies it passes through.
- Base Damage: 40-60 cold per pulse
- Freeze chance: 20% base
- Projectile speed: fast
- Gem Quality Bonus: +1% freeze duration per quality level

#### Ice Nova
- Type: Spell, AoE, Cold, Nova
- Description: Detonates a ring of ice around caster or a nearby Frostbolt.
- Base Damage: 80-120 cold
- Ring radius: 2.0 meters
- Chill on hit: 30% action speed reduction for 2 seconds
- Gem Quality Bonus: +0.5% radius per quality level

#### Arctic Breath
- Type: Spell, Projectile, Cold, AoE, Duration
- Description: Fires a slow-moving cold projectile that leaves a trail of chilled ground.
- Ground chill duration: 3 seconds
- Projectile speed: very slow
- Gem Quality Bonus: +1% cold damage per quality level

#### Cold Snap
- Type: Spell, AoE, Cold, Duration
- Description: Creates a burst of cold in an area and leaves a chilled zone.
- Burst damage: 100-150 cold
- Zone duration: 4 seconds
- Zone chill: 20% action speed reduction
- Gem Quality Bonus: +2% chill magnitude per quality level

### Lightning Damage Skills

#### Lightning Strike
- Type: Attack, Melee, Projectile, Lightning
- Description: Melee strike that also fires projectiles toward enemies.
- Melee Damage: 100% of weapon damage (physical + lightning)
- Projectile count: 3
- Projectile damage: 60% of weapon damage
- Gem Quality Bonus: +1 projectile per 10 quality levels

#### Arc
- Type: Spell, Chaining, Lightning, Duration
- Description: Fires a lightning bolt that chains between enemies.
- Chain count: 7 base
- Damage per chain: reduces by 10%
- Shock chance: 20% base
- Gem Quality Bonus: +0.5% chain damage per quality level

#### Ball Lightning
- Type: Spell, Projectile, AoE, Lightning, Duration
- Description: Fires a slow-moving ball that repeatedly pulses lightning in its area.
- Pulse frequency: once per 0.2 seconds
- Pulse damage: 20-30 lightning per pulse
- Ball duration: 2.0 seconds
- Gem Quality Bonus: +5% increased ball lightning duration per quality level

#### Discharge
- Type: Spell, AoE, Lightning, Fire, Cold, Duration
- Description: Consumes all active charges on the player and releases them as elemental damage.
- Damage scales with charges consumed; endurance charges add fire, frenzy charges add cold, power charges add lightning.
- Gem Quality Bonus: +2% damage per quality level

---

### Chaos Damage Skills

#### Poisonous Concoction
- Type: Attack, Projectile, Chaos, AoE
- Description: Throw a flask of poison that deals chaos damage on impact.
- Poison chance: 100%
- Base poison stacks applied: 3
- Damage scales with life flask quality and life pool
- Gem Quality Bonus: +1 poison stack per quality level

#### Bane
- Type: Spell, Chaos, Duration, AoE
- Description: Applies linked curses and deals chaos damage to affected enemies.
- Base Damage: 50-75 chaos
- Duration: 5 seconds
- Applies up to 2 linked curse gems
- Gem Quality Bonus: +1% chaos damage per quality level

#### Essence Drain
- Type: Spell, Projectile, Chaos, Duration
- Description: Fires a projectile that deals chaos damage over time; regenerates life on each tick.
- Life regeneration per tick: 1.5% of dealt damage
- DoT damage: 100-150 chaos over 8 seconds
- Gem Quality Bonus: +1% DoT damage per quality level

#### Contagion
- Type: Spell, AoE, Chaos, Duration
- Description: Spreads chaos damage over time to nearby enemies when they die.
- Spread radius: 2.5 meters
- Duration of spread: 3 seconds
- Damage: 40-60 chaos over time
- Gem Quality Bonus: +0.5% spread radius per quality level

## Support Gem Reference

### Universal Supports

#### Added Fire Damage
- Adds 10-16 fire damage to linked skills.
- Mana cost multiplier: 120%
- Compatible with: attacks and spells.

#### Faster Attacks
- 20% increased attack speed for linked skills.
- Mana cost multiplier: 110%

#### Faster Casting
- 15% increased cast speed for linked skills.
- Mana cost multiplier: 110%

#### Spell Echo
- Linked spell repeats once with 60% less damage.
- Mana cost multiplier: 140%

#### Multistrike
- Linked attack repeats 2 additional times at 66% damage each.
- Mana cost multiplier: 140%

#### Melee Splash
- Linked melee attack hits nearby enemies for 60% of damage.
- Mana cost multiplier: 130%

#### Fortify
- Linked melee attack applies Fortify on hit; reduces damage taken by 20%.
- Mana cost multiplier: 120%

#### Concentrated Effect
- 50% more area damage; 35% reduced area of effect.
- Mana cost multiplier: 140%

#### Increased Area of Effect
- 20% more area of effect.
- Mana cost multiplier: 130%

#### Brutality
- 40% more physical damage; cannot deal non-physical damage.
- Mana cost multiplier: 140%

#### Controlled Destruction
- 40% more spell damage; 100% reduced critical strike chance.
- Mana cost multiplier: 140%

## Aura Gems

### Auras that Affect the Player

#### Grace
- Grants 1,000+ evasion rating.
- Mana reservation: 35%.

#### Determination
- Grants 1,000+ armor.
- Mana reservation: 35%.

#### Discipline
- Grants 200+ energy shield.
- Mana reservation: 35%.

#### Haste
- 15% increased attack speed, cast speed, and movement speed.
- Mana reservation: 50%.

#### Anger
- Adds 30-60 fire damage to attacks.
- Mana reservation: 50%.

#### Wrath
- Adds 30-50 lightning damage to attacks.
- Mana reservation: 50%.

#### Hatred
- 18% of physical damage added as cold damage.
- Mana reservation: 50%.

#### Zealotry
- 25% more spell damage; consecrated ground around you.
- Mana reservation: 50%.

### Auras that Affect Enemies

#### Punishment
- Enemies near you take 20% increased damage.
- Mana reservation: 35%.

#### Vulnerability
- Enemies near you have 20% reduced armor; 10% chance to apply bleed from attacks.
- Mana reservation: 35%.

---

## Gem Leveling Notes

- All active skill gems can be leveled to level 20 by using them in combat.
- Level 20 gems can be corrupted with a Vaal Orb to attempt level 21 (5% chance) or quality 23% (5% chance).
- Support gems also level to 20; their quality bonuses are often more impactful than levels.
