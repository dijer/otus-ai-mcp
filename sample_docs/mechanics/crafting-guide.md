# Path of Exile 2: Crafting System Complete Guide

## Overview

Crafting in Path of Exile 2 allows players to modify items using currency orbs. The crafting system ranges from simple magic item rolls to deterministic multi-mod endgame crafting.

---

## Item Tiers and Rarities

### Normal Items
- Zero modifiers.
- Can be upgraded to magic with Orb of Transmutation.
- Can be upgraded to rare with Orb of Alchemy.

### Magic Items
- 1-2 modifiers (1 prefix + 1 suffix maximum).
- Can be rerolled with Orb of Alteration.
- Can have a modifier added with Orb of Augmentation.

### Rare Items
- 3-6 modifiers (up to 3 prefixes + 3 suffixes).
- Rerolled with Chaos Orb.
- One modifier added with Exalted Orb.
- One modifier removed with Orb of Annulment.

### Unique Items
- Fixed explicit modifiers.
- Cannot be modified except by corrupting with a Vaal Orb.

---

## Primary Crafting Currency

### Orb of Transmutation
- Effect: Upgrades normal item to magic (1-2 mods).
- Primary use: Magic item base crafting start.
- Trade value: 1 Transmutation = 1/4 Alteration roughly.

### Orb of Alteration
- Effect: Rerolls all modifiers on a magic item.
- Primary use: Spam-rolling for specific 2-mod combos on flasks, jewelry.
- Trade value: Bulk commodity; commonly available.

### Orb of Augmentation
- Effect: Adds one modifier to a magic item that has fewer than 2.
- Primary use: Adding a second mod after hitting a desired first mod.

### Chaos Orb
- Effect: Rerolls all modifiers on a rare item.
- Primary use: General rare item rerolling.
- Trade currency standard; most item prices expressed in Chaos Orbs.

### Exalted Orb
- Effect: Adds one random modifier to a rare item with fewer than 6.
- Primary use: Adding a final modifier to a near-perfect rare.
- Trade value: Approximately 150-180 Chaos Orbs.
- Risk: If the item is full (6 mods), Exalted Orb cannot be used.

### Orb of Annulment
- Effect: Removes one random modifier from a magic or rare item.
- Primary use: Removing a single bad mod from an otherwise good rare.
- Risk: Random; can remove a desired modifier.
- Trade value: Approximately 15-25 Chaos Orbs.

### Divine Orb
- Effect: Re-rolls the numeric values of all existing modifiers within their ranges.
- Primary use: Perfecting the values of an item that has the right modifier types.
- Trade value: Approximately 200-250 Chaos Orbs.
- Does not change which modifiers are present.

### Mirror of Kalandra
- Effect: Creates an exact copy of any non-unique, non-corrupted item.
- Primary use: Duplicating BiS (Best in Slot) items.
- Trade value: Approximately 200,000 Chaos Orbs.
- Rarest currency item in the game.

## Advanced Crafting Methods

### Fossil Crafting
- Fossils are placed in Resonators (1-4 socket) to modify crafting outcomes.
- Each fossil biases crafting toward or away from specific modifier categories.

#### Common Fossils

**Scorched Fossil**
- Effect: More fire modifiers; fewer cold modifiers.
- Best use: Crafting fire damage jewelry or weapons.

**Frigid Fossil**
- Effect: More cold modifiers; fewer fire modifiers.
- Best use: Crafting cold damage jewelry or weapons.

**Jagged Fossil**
- Effect: More physical modifiers; fewer elemental modifiers.
- Best use: Physical damage weapons.

**Dense Fossil**
- Effect: More defensive modifiers; fewer offensive modifiers.
- Best use: Crafting life-heavy armors.

**Pristine Fossil**
- Effect: More life modifiers; fewer chaos and currency modifiers.
- Best use: High life body armor or jewelry.

**Sanctified Fossil**
- Effect: Numeric values of modifiers are 30% higher than normal.
- Best use: End-stage crafting when the modifier is determined but values are low.
- Trade value: Very high; one of the rarest fossils.
- Verification Fact: Sanctified Fossil increases modifier numeric values by exactly 30%.

**Corroded Fossil**
- Effect: More chaos modifiers; fewer physical modifiers.
- Best use: Chaos damage builds.

**Bound Fossil**
- Effect: More minion modifiers; fewer other modifiers.
- Best use: Necromancer gear.

### Essence Crafting
- Essences guarantee one specific modifier when used on an item.
- The item is otherwise random.
- Essences of higher tier (Deafening) guarantee higher value ranges of the specific mod.

#### Notable Essences

**Essence of Delirium**
- Guarantees: Players and Minions have 3% Chance to deal Triple Damage.
- Tier: Deafening.
- Use: Top-tier minion builds.

**Essence of Envy**
- Guarantees: Adds X chaos damage (value scales with tier).
- Tier: Shrieking or Deafening.
- Use: Chaos damage builds.

**Essence of Contempt**
- Guarantees: Adds X-Y physical damage to attacks (value scales with tier).
- Tier: Multiple tiers available.
- Use: Physical attack builds.

**Essence of Loathing**
- Guarantees: Enemies you kill explode, dealing 3% of their life as physical damage.
- Tier: Deafening.
- Use: Clearing-focused builds.

## Crafting Bench (Hideout)

### Overview
- The Crafting Bench is found in the player's Hideout.
- It allows applying specific modifiers to items for a currency cost.
- Modifiers are unlocked by completing maps that contain specific Masters.

### Common Crafting Bench Modifiers

**Life Modifiers**
- +30-39 to maximum life: 1 Orb of Alteration.
- +40-49 to maximum life: 2 Orb of Alteration.
- +50-59 to maximum life: 5 Chaos Orb.

**Resistance Modifiers**
- +30-35% to fire resistance: 1 Orb of Alteration.
- +30-35% to all elemental resistances: 1 Exalted Orb.

**Damage Modifiers**
- +1 to level of socketed gems: 2 Exalted Orb.
- Adds 10-14 physical damage: 3 Orb of Alteration.

**Multi-Mod Crafting**
- "Can have up to 3 Crafted Modifiers": 2 Exalted Orbs.
- Unlocks the ability to stack up to 3 Bench modifiers simultaneously.
- Standard endgame crafting method for finishing rare items.

## Currency Sink Systems

### Vaal Orb
- Corrupts an item, randomly applying one of several outcomes.
- Outcomes on items: add implicit modifier, change implicit, add random modifier, remove modifier.
- Outcome on skill gems: +1 level, -1 level, +1 quality, -1 quality.
- All corrupted items cannot be further modified.

### Blessed Orb
- Re-rolls values of implicit modifiers only (not explicit).
- Primary use: Improving base implicit values on jewelry.

### Orb of Scouring
- Removes all modifiers from a magic or rare item, returning it to normal.
- Primary use: Resetting a failed craft to start over.

## Beginner Crafting Strategy (Acts 1-6)

1. Pick up all rare items and identify them.
2. Use Chaos Orbs only on items with good base types.
3. Use the Crafting Bench for life and resistance filling once Act 4+.
4. Do not use Divine Orbs until you have confirmed all modifiers are correct.

## Intermediate Crafting Strategy (Early Maps)

1. Target specific base items (ilvl 80+ for top-tier mods).
2. Use Fossils to bias toward desired modifier categories.
3. Use Bench to fill one or two remaining mod slots.
4. Aim for 5 good modifiers on a 6-mod rare.

## Advanced Crafting Strategy (Endgame)

1. Use Awakener's Orb to combine two influenced items.
2. Apply "Can have up to 3 Crafted Modifiers" to allow stacking bench mods.
3. Use Sanctuary prefix crafting to fill specific slots without risk.
4. Divine Orb only after confirming all modifier types are ideal.

---

## Crafting Efficiency Tips

### Resistance Capping Efficiently
- Use Crafting Bench resistance modifiers to cap all resistances for free before buying gear.
- Target: 75% all elements; 0%+ chaos resistance minimum.

### Jewel Crafting
- Jewels can only be rolled with Alterations and Chaos Orbs; no Bench options.
- 4-mod jewels with two damage modifiers and one life modifier are the target.

### Flask Crafting
- Flask implicits are set by flask type; only suffixes can be modified.
- Use Orb of Alteration to roll for desired anti-ailment (bleed, freeze, poison removal) or buff suffix.

---

## Verification Facts
- Sanctified Fossil increases modifier numeric values by exactly 30%.
- "Can have up to 3 Crafted Modifiers" bench option costs exactly 2 Exalted Orbs.
- Orb of Annulment removes exactly one random modifier; it cannot target specific modifiers.
- Divine Orb re-rolls all modifier values within their ranges without changing which modifiers are present.
- Blessed Orb affects only implicit modifiers, not explicit ones.
