# Path of Exile 2: Build Archetype Guide

## Overview

This guide covers the major build archetypes in Path of Exile 2, their core mechanics, gear requirements, and boss encounter performance.

---

## 1. Bleed Gladiator

### Overview
- One of the most straightforward endgame builds.
- Applies bleed stacks through rapid physical attacks.
- Damage scales with enemy movement; works best against bosses that charge and dash.

### Core Skills
- Lacerate: primary bleed applicator.
- Puncture: high-bleed single target.
- Bladestorm: creates persistent spinning blades around the player.
- Whirling Blades: movement skill.

### Passive Tree Focus
- Bleed clusters: Crimson Dance (allows 8 bleed stacks instead of 1).
- Life clusters: target 5,500-6,500 life.
- Critical strike optional; Crimson Dance requires no crits for full stacks.

### Gear Priority
- Weapon: Two-handed sword or axe with physical damage and attack speed.
- Unique option: Vexing Thorn for bonus bleed chance.
- Armor: Life + Armor combination; target 20,000+ armor with Granite Flask.

### Boss Performance
- Excellent against: Ironclad Ravager (mobile boss), Hollow General Draev.
- Weak against: Bosses that are frequently airborne or have bleed immunity phases.
- Counters: Crimson Dance ensures full bleed stacks even during brief boss movements.

### Key Stats to Target
- Life: 5,500 minimum; 7,000+ for red maps.
- Bleed damage: 300%+ increased.
- Physical damage: 250%+ increased.
- Attack speed: 2.5+ attacks per second.

## 2. Fireball Elementalist (Witch)

### Overview
- High burst fire spell caster.
- Focuses on ignite proliferation and fireball overlap damage.
- Most effective in dense content but also strong on single-target with proper scaling.

### Core Skills
- Fireball: primary skill.
- Arcane Surge: buff for increased spell damage.
- Clarity: mana regeneration aura.
- Flammability: fire resistance reduction curse.

### Passive Tree Focus
- Spell damage clusters: target 300%+ increased spell damage.
- Energy shield: secondary defense layer; target 4,000+ ES.
- Elemental damage clusters near Witch starting area.

### Gear Priority
- Weapon: Wand or scepter with spell damage and fire damage.
- Chest: Energy shield base; Shroud of Madness if chaos damage is a concern.
- Accessories: Rings and amulet with spell damage, fire damage, life, and mana.

### Boss Performance
- Excellent against: Ashen Magistrate (fire damage amplified by his own Scorch mechanic).
- Weak against: Fire immune or fire absorb bosses.
- Counters: Flammability curse reduces boss fire resistance by 25%.

### Key Stats to Target
- Energy shield: 4,000 minimum; 6,000+ for pinnacle.
- Fire damage: 400%+ increased spell damage.
- Cast speed: 3.0+ casts per second.
- Ignite chance: 80%+ for reliable ignite uptime.

## 3. Poison Assassin (Shadow)

### Overview
- DoT-based chaos damage build.
- Applies multiple poison stacks per hit through Poisonous Concoction.
- Very smooth scaling; easy to transition from acts to endgame.

### Core Skills
- Poisonous Concoction: primary skill.
- Withering Step: applies wither debuff (increased chaos damage taken).
- Blink: movement skill.
- Despair: chaos resistance reduction curse.

### Passive Tree Focus
- Poison clusters: Pathological (increases maximum poison stacks to 24), Deadly Infusion.
- Dexterity and intelligence hybrid for tree efficiency.
- Life: target 4,500-5,500 life.

### Gear Priority
- Weapon: None required (Poisonous Concoction scales from flasks and life).
- Flask: Life flask with as high quality as possible; T1 mod preferred.
- Chest: Life + evasion combination.

### Boss Performance
- Excellent against: Stationary bosses in stagger lock windows.
- Weak against: Chaos immune bosses, bosses with frequent teleport phases.
- Counters: Despair curse dramatically accelerates poison damage.

### Key Stats to Target
- Life: 4,500 minimum.
- Chaos damage: 300%+ increased.
- Poison stacks: 20+ maintained.
- Movement speed: 30%+ for safety.

## 4. Minion Necromancer (Witch)

### Overview
- Passive damage dealer through raised skeletons and zombies.
- Player focuses entirely on positioning and survivability.
- Scales very well into endgame without expensive gear.

### Core Skills
- Summon Skeleton Warriors: primary offensive minion.
- Summon Skeleton Mages: ranged fire supplement.
- Raise Zombie: meat shield minion.
- Convocation: recalls all minions to player position.

### Passive Tree Focus
- Minion damage clusters.
- Minion life clusters.
- Player life: 4,000 minimum (you take damage too).

### Gear Priority
- Weapon: Wand or shield; look for minion damage or summoning bonuses.
- Amulet: Minion damage + minion life.
- Unique option: Any item that grants additional skeleton summon limit.

### Boss Performance
- Excellent against: All encounter types; minions distribute threat.
- Weak against: Enemies that prioritize summon targeting with cleave effects.
- Counters: Convocation repositions minions instantly to counter boss movement.

### Key Stats to Target
- Minion count: 10+ active skeletons.
- Minion damage: 200%+ increased.
- Player life: 4,000+.

## 5. Archmage Hierophant (Templar)

### Overview
- Mana-scaling spell build where mana pool directly increases spell damage.
- Requires significant investment in mana pool and regeneration.
- One of the highest skill cap builds; rewards proper mana management.

### Core Skills
- Archmage Support: converts mana spent into spell damage bonus.
- Ball Lightning or Arc: primary spell.
- Clarity: mana regeneration aura.
- Mind Over Matter: passive keystone (mana absorbs 30% of damage).

### Passive Tree Focus
- Mana clusters: target 6,000+ mana.
- Mana regeneration: 300+ mana per second.
- Life for damage absorption.

### Gear Priority
- All gear must have mana modifiers.
- Ideal mana pool: 6,000-10,000.
- Archmage support at level 20 provides +3 spell damage per 100 mana.

### Boss Performance
- Excellent against: Single-target bosses during long punish windows.
- Weak against: Encounters that require constant movement (interrupts channeled spells).
- Counters: Large mana pool enables extremely high burst windows.

### Key Stats to Target
- Mana: 6,000 minimum; 10,000 for optimal Archmage scaling.
- Mana regeneration: 300+ per second.
- Spell damage: scales automatically with mana pool.

## 6. Earthquake Berserker (Warrior)

### Overview
- Ground-slam build using Earthquake and aftershock mechanics.
- Every Earthquake creates an aftershock 1.6 seconds later.
- High damage but slow; requires positioning to land both hit and aftershock.

### Core Skills
- Earthquake: primary skill.
- Enduring Cry: warcry for endurance charges and rage.
- Leap Slam: movement skill.
- Molten Shell: protective active skill.

### Passive Tree Focus
- Stagger clusters.
- Life and armor clusters.
- Rage generation and consumption for Berserker.

### Gear Priority
- Weapon: Two-handed mace or maul with area damage or physical damage.
- Target: 500+ physical damage per hit.
- Life: 7,000+ for Berserker survivability.

### Boss Performance
- Excellent against: Slow bosses with predictable patterns.
- Weak against: Mobile bosses that move off aftershock locations.
- Counters: Stagger lock applies during full-boss hit sequences.

### Key Stats to Target
- Life: 7,000 minimum; 9,000+ for pinnacle content.
- Physical damage: 500%+ increased.
- Area damage: 150%+ increased (for wider aftershock coverage).

---

## 7. Storm Brand Inquisitor (Templar)

### Overview
- Branded-area lightning spell build.
- Places brands on enemies; brands activate automatically when nearby.
- Very safe playstyle: player can dodge while brands deal damage.

### Core Skills
- Storm Brand: primary skill.
- Brand Recall: recalls all brands to nearest enemy.
- Conductivity: lightning resistance reduction curse.
- Arcane Surge: spell damage buff.

### Passive Tree Focus
- Brand duration and frequency clusters.
- Lightning and elemental damage.
- Energy shield secondary mitigation.

### Gear Priority
- Weapon: Wand with spell damage and lightning damage.
- Shield: Energy shield; look for regeneration.
- Gloves: Attack speed irrelevant; prioritize life/ES and resists.

### Boss Performance
- Excellent against: Bosses that move predictably; brands follow the boss.
- Weak against: Bosses that teleport away from brands frequently.
- Counters: Brand Recall instantly repositions all brands.

### Key Stats to Target
- Brand duration: 3+ seconds.
- Lightning damage: 400%+ increased.
- Energy shield: 3,500+ minimum.
