# Path of Exile 2: Quest and Achievement Systems

## Part 1: Quest Types and Rewards

### 1.1 Story Quest System

**Overview**:
- Total quests: 60+ across acts 1-6
- Completion: mandatory to progress (can't skip)
- Rewards: skill gems, passive points, stat bonuses

**Act 1 Quests** (10 total):
1. A Fallen Warrior (kill Brutus) → reward: Raise Zombie gem
2. The Caged Brute (kill imprisoned beast) → reward: Added Cold Damage
3. Mercy Mission (escort NPC to shore) → reward: Dexterity +10
4. The False Ascendant (kill Merveil) → reward: Act 2 access
5. Deal with the Bandits (choose alliance) → reward: +2 passive or skill gem

**Quest Reward Scaling**:
- Early quests (act 1): small rewards (1 passive point)
- Mid quests (acts 2-4): moderate rewards (2 passive points + gem)
- Late quests (acts 5-6): major rewards (3 passive points + unique item)

### 1.2 Side Quests and Optional Content

**Unlockable Quests**:
- Labyrinth trials: complete 6 trials to unlock ascendancy lab
- Defeat Izaro (labyrinth boss): rewards passive points + ascendancy access
- Defeat Kitava (final boss): unlocks endgame

**Optional Dungeons**:
- Pyramid Labyrinth (Act 3): optional, grants lab trial completion
- Hidden caves: contain unique items, no mandatory rewards
- Event arenas: optional PvE tournaments

## Part 2: Achievement Systems

### 2.1 Challenge League Achievements

**Seasonal Challenges** (4-month league):
- Total: 40 challenges per season
- Tiered rewards: 12 challenges = cosmetic reward 1, 24 challenges = cosmetic 2, 40 challenges = exclusive cosmetic

**Challenge Examples**:
1. "Kill 50 unique bosses" → collect unique drops
2. "Reach map tier 16" → complete endgame
3. "Accumulate 1,000 chaos" → farm currency
4. "Complete hardcore without death" → survive fully
5. "Defeat all ascendancy bosses" → specialized boss farming

**Seasonal Structure**:
- Weeks 1-2: easy challenges (reachable by casual players)
- Weeks 3-8: medium challenges (require farming/strategy)
- Weeks 8-12: hard challenges (extreme time/skill investment)

### 2.2 Hardcore-Specific Achievements

**Hardcore Ladder Achievements**:
- "First to level 90 in hardcore" (awarded to 1 player/season)
- "Reach tier 16 maps in hardcore" (top 0.1%)
- "Survive 30 consecutive maps without death" (skill check)
- "Defeat all endgame bosses in single hardcore character" (extreme endurance)

**Reward Structure**:
- Cosmetic cosmetics (character skins, weapon skins)
- Titles ("Hardcore Champion", "Map Master")
- Tournament invitations (for top achievers)

### 2.3 Account-Wide Achievements

**Permanent Achievements** (carry across characters):
- "Defeat all act bosses" (1-time unlock)
- "Reach level 100 character" (extremely rare)
- "Craft perfect item (6 mod maxroll)" (technical skill)
- "Kill Searing Exarch (endgame pinnacle)" (high difficulty)

**Completion Status**:
- Each player can view achievement progress (% completion)
- Leaderboard: show rank of players by achievement completion
- Exclusive rewards: certain cosmetics require specific achievements

## Part 3: Atlas Completion and Endgame Progression

### 3.1 Atlas Structure

**Map Grid**:
- Total maps: 125 (5 per tier, 25 tiers total including unique maps)
- Tier range: 1-16 (1 = easiest white maps, 16 = hardest red maps)
- Progression: unlock higher tiers by completing lower tiers

**Atlas Completion Bonuses**:
- Per map completed: +0.8% item rarity (global, permanent)
- Tier 1 complete (5 maps): +4% rarity, unlock tier 2
- Tier 16 complete (5 maps): +4% rarity, maximum benefit

**Total Bonus** (all 125 maps):
- Item rarity: +100% (doubles rare drop rate)
- Experience gain: +20% (speeds leveling)
- Map drop chance: +100% (sustains farming)

### 3.2 Map Completion Mechanics

**Unique Maps**:
- Special maps with custom mechanics (blight, abyss, breach)
- Completion grants extra atlas passive points
- Examples: "Strand" (physical DPS map), "Lair of the Hydra" (cold immunity map)

**Watchstone Mechanics**:
- 4 watchstones placed on atlas (one per conqueror region)
- Each watchstone: unlocks specific region and conqueror passives
- Acquisition: defeat conqueror bosses (Baran, Veritania, Drox, Al-Hezmin)

**Conqueror Progression**:
- Farm maps to spawn conqueror encounters
- Defeat conqueror → gain watchstone
- All 4 watchstones → access Elder/Maven content (end-endgame)

### 3.3 Endgame Boss Content

**Endgame Pinnacle Bosses**:
1. **Searing Exarch** (fire-based pinnacle)
   - Requirement: farm specific maps with sextants
   - Drops: exclusive unique items worth 500+ chaos
   - Difficulty: requires 3,500 life + fire resistance 80%+

2. **Eater of Worlds** (chaos/void-based pinnacle)
   - Requirement: farm different set of maps
   - Drops: exclusive unique items (void-element themed)
   - Difficulty: requires chaos resistance 50%+ + 3,000 life

3. **The Shaper** (classic boss, legacy)
   - Requirement: farm shaped maps (specific atlas path)
   - Drops: legacy unique items (valuable, rare)
   - Difficulty: ultra-high mechanic complexity

4. **Maven** (super-boss)
   - Requirement: defeat all pinnacle bosses + unlock Maven
   - Drops: unique jewels (enable build archetypes)
   - Difficulty: combines mechanics of all preceding bosses

### 3.4 Beyond Endgame (Mirror-Tier Content)

**Mirror of Kalandra**:
- Unique currency that duplicates perfect items
- Drops: <0.00001% chance (extremely rare)
- Value: 200,000+ chaos (most expensive item in game)
- Cosmetic: displays character name on item (permanent immortality)

**Synthesis Content**:
- Legacy endgame mechanic (no longer in game)
- Mentioned for completeness (historical interest)

## Part 4: Seasonal Leaderboards

### 4.1 Leaderboard Categories

**Speed Leaderboards**:
1. "First to Level 70" (act 6 completion)
2. "First to Level 90" (endgame mastery)
3. "First to level 100" (supreme achievement)

**Wealth Leaderboards**:
1. "Most Chaos Farmed" (currency accumulation)
2. "Most Unique Items Found" (drop rate luck)
3. "Highest Item Rarity" (crafting perfection)

**Hardcore Leaderboards**:
1. "Longest Hardcore Survival" (character age in days)
2. "Highest Hardcore Level Achieved" (pure endurance)
3. "Most Hardcore Kills Without Death" (kill count on single char)

### 4.2 Seasonal Competition

**Top 10 Finishers**:
- Rank 1: 2,000 chaos + exclusive cosmetic
- Rank 2: 1,500 chaos + exclusive cosmetic
- Rank 3: 1,000 chaos + cosmetic
- Rank 4-10: 500 chaos + cosmetic

**Sponsorship Eligibility**:
- Top 50 players in leaderboards (across categories)
- Sponsorship offers: $500-5,000/month from companies
- Tournament circuit: seasonal invitational tournaments (500k chaos prize pools)

### 4.3 Leaderboard Meta

**Dominant Strategies** (previous seasons):
- Season 1: Earthquake Berserker (simple, efficient)
- Season 2: Spell Echo Wizard (burst damage, fast clear)
- Season 3: Poison Assassin (DoT scaling, endurance)
- Season 4: Block Paladin (survive first, offense second)

**Shifting Meta**:
- Developers nerf previous season's top build
- Players innovate new strategies to adapt
- Meta diversity maintained through balance patches

## Part 5: Daily and Weekly Systems

### 5.1 Daily Quests

**Daily Bonus Quest** (one per day):
- Requirement: complete any map
- Reward: 50 chaos equivalent in drops
- Timer: resets at 10 AM UTC daily

**Incentive Structure**:
- Casual: 50 chaos/day = 350 chaos/week (sustains farming)
- Hardcore: daily runs become mandatory (FOMO - fear of missing out)
- Seasonal: daily rewards add 1,500+ chaos/month (significant income)

### 5.2 Weekly Events

**Weekly Boss Rush**:
- Kill 5 unique bosses in sequence
- Time limit: 30 minutes total
- Reward: exclusive cosmetic (seasonal only)
- Difficulty: requires optimized build and skill

**Weekly Gauntlet**:
- Survive 10 consecutive waves of enemies
- Each wave: +20% difficulty
- Reward: XP + currency scaling with wave count
- Hardcore variant: permadeath on any death

### 5.3 Seasonal Events

**Mid-League Events** (week 6):
- Special boss encounter (unique mechanic)
- Double rewards for 1 week
- Seasonal challenge bonus (extra points)

**End-League Events** (week 10-12):
- Grand finale tournament (1,000+ player bracket)
- Finals broadcast on Twitch (spectator reward cosmetics)
- Championship title + prize pool distribution

## Part 6: Cosmetics and Cosmetic Shop

### 6.1 Cosmetic Categories

**Character Cosmetics**:
- Armor skins: change appearance of equipped gear
- Weapon skins: cosmetic weapon appearance
- Aura effects: modify passive effect visuals
- Pet cosmetics: add companion to character

**Cost**:
- Standard cosmetics: 10-20 USD equivalent
- Exclusive cosmetics: 30-50 USD (limited edition)
- Bundle cosmetics: 50-100 USD (themed packages)

### 6.2 Cosmetic Acquisition

**Seasonal Achievement Cosmetics** (free):
- Earned by completing challenges (40 challenges = cosmetic worth $20)
- Non-tradeable (account-bound only)
- Status symbol: publicly visible achievement marker

**Battle Pass Cosmetics**:
- Season pass: $10-20 for cosmetics + currency rewards
- 10-level tiers: each tier = cosmetic + currency
- Free track: minimal cosmetics available without purchase

### 6.3 Cosmetic Economy

**Secondary Market**:
- Trading posts: players resell cosmetics (sometimes 2x markup)
- Speculation: limited-edition cosmetics increase in value
- Collector's value: rare cosmetics worth 100+ USD on resale

## Conclusion

Quest and achievement systems in Path of Exile 2 provide:
1. **Progression framework** (mandatory story quests)
2. **Optional challenges** (achievements for dedicated players)
3. **Seasonal engagement** (leaderboards, events, rewards)
4. **Long-term goals** (cosmetics, titles, sponsorship)

The layered system accommodates casual players (story completion) and hardcore competitors (leaderboard grinding) equally, creating broad appeal.
