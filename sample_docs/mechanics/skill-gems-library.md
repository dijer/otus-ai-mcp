# Path of Exile 2: Comprehensive Skill Gem Library and Support Combinations

## Part 1: Active Skill Gems Reference

### 1.1 Physical Attack Skills

**Earthquake** (signature ability)
- Damage Type: pure physical
- Attack Type: two-handed attack
- Scaling: +physical damage, +attack speed
- Mechanic: detonates after 1.6 seconds, area damage = 150% initial hit
- Gem Level 1: 100 physical damage, 0.8 second cast time
- Gem Level 20: 550 physical damage, 0.6 second cast time
- DPS: 917 at gem level 20
- Use Case: primary skill for Berserker, Warrior

**Cyclone** (mobility skill)
- Damage Type: pure physical (scales attack damage)
- Skill Type: movement attack
- Mechanic: spin while moving, hit all nearby enemies
- Gem Level 1: 80 physical damage, 0.8 second wind-up
- Gem Level 20: 450 physical damage, 0.5 second wind-up
- DPS: 900 (with 2.0 attack speed)
- Use Case: map clear, boss tank build

**Cleave** (beginner skill)
- Damage Type: pure physical
- Attack Type: one-handed or two-handed
- Mechanic: swings in cone, hits all enemies in range
- Gem Level 1: 60 physical damage, 0.7 second swing time
- Gem Level 20: 320 physical damage, 0.5 second swing time
- DPS: 640
- Use Case: early game, transition to Earthquake

**Reave** (finesse skill)
- Damage Type: pure physical with elemental scaling options
- Attack Type: dual-wield daggers or swords
- Mechanic: builds stacks on hit, damage scales with stacks
- Gem Level 1: 40 base damage, +20 per stack (max 8 stacks)
- Gem Level 20: 220 base, +110 per stack
- Max DPS: 220 + (110 × 8) = 1,100 with full stacks
- Use Case: crit builds, conversion scaling

### 1.2 Spell Skills

**Fireball** (beginner spell)
- Damage Type: fire (elemental)
- Casting Type: projectile spell
- Gem Level 1: 100 fire damage, 0.7 second cast time
- Gem Level 20: 550 fire damage, 0.5 second cast time
- DPS: 1,100
- Mechanic: projectile explodes on impact or after 2 seconds
- Use Case: act 1-3, beginner friendly

**Incinerate** (advanced spell)
- Damage Type: fire (pure elemental)
- Casting Type: channeled spell
- Gem Level 1: 70 fire damage base, up to 5 stages = 350%
- Gem Level 20: 400 fire damage base, 5 stages = 2,000 final
- DPS: 4,000 at gem level 20 (stage 5 sustained)
- Mechanic: stage scaling, each stage = +70% damage
- Use Case: Templar, fire DoT builds, endgame spells

**Ice Spear** (projectile spell)
- Damage Type: cold (elemental)
- Casting Type: projectile spell
- Gem Level 1: 80 cold damage, projectile converts to piercing after distance
- Gem Level 20: 450 cold damage
- DPS: 900
- Mechanic: pierces distant enemies, freezes
- Use Case: cold builds, freeze-focused strategies

**Vaal Fireball** (ultimate spell)
- Damage Type: fire
- Casting Type: vaal skill (uses vaal souls)
- Gem Level 1: 200 fire damage, area effect, 40 vaal souls cost
- Gem Level 20: 1,100 fire damage
- DPS: 1,100 per cast (expensive, situational)
- Mechanic: requires vaal souls from kills
- Use Case: burst damage windows, boss phases

### 1.3 Utility Skills

**Frenzy Charge Generator** (specific to ascendancy)
- Type: passive skill activation
- Mechanic: generates frenzy charges on kill (Berserker only)
- Effect: each charge = +10% attack/cast speed, +10% damage
- Max charges: 3 (30% bonus when full)
- Duration: until death or when reset
- Use Case: maintain constant damage buff

**Movement Skills** (Dash, Teleport)
- Dash: short-range blink, 1-second cooldown
- Teleport: targeted blink, 0.8-second cooldown
- Use: escape danger, position for damage

## Part 2: Support Gem Combinations

### 2.1 Damage Support Gems

**Melee Physical Damage** (primary scaling)
- Added to: all physical attacks
- Bonus: +30% physical damage
- Cost: 1 socket in support slot
- Synergy: stacks multiplicatively with gear scaling
- Example: 100 damage × 1.3 (support) = 130 damage

**Added Fire Damage** (elemental conversion)
- Added to: physical attacks (converts to hybrid)
- Bonus: +50 flat fire damage per gem level
- Gem level 20: +1,000 flat fire damage to all attacks
- Synergy: enables elemental scaling (50% phys + 50% fire)
- Example: 100 phys + 500 fire = 600 total, scales both

**Multistrike** (attack multiplier)
- Added to: attack skills only
- Bonus: repeat attack 2 additional times at 66% damage
- Total DPS: 100% + 66% + 66% = 232% (2.32x multiplier)
- Cost: medium (requires 3 sockets minimum)
- Use: highest DPS multiplier for attack builds

**Spell Echo** (spell multiplier)
- Added to: spell skills only
- Bonus: repeat spell with 60% less damage
- Total DPS: 100% + 60% = 160% (1.6x multiplier)
- Cost: moderate (2 socket minimum)
- Synergy: enables multi-projectile stacking

**Increased Area of Effect** (map clear)
- Added to: area skills (Earthquake, Fireball)
- Bonus: +30% skill area size
- Effect: hits more enemies per cast
- Scaling: DPS increases by enemy count hit
- Use Case: map farming (more enemies = more damage)

### 2.2 Utility Support Gems

**Fortify** (damage mitigation)
- Added to: physical attacks
- Bonus: +20% damage reduction (multiplicative)
- Duration: 5 seconds (refreshed on hit)
- Cost: 1 socket (mandatory for tank builds)
- Synergy: stacks with armor, making mitigation extreme

**Life Leech** (sustainability)
- Added to: attack skills
- Bonus: 2-3% of damage dealt returns as life
- Cap: capped at 2× base leech rate by attack speed
- Use: enables infinite sustain in endgame combat
- Requirement: 4,000+ DPS to sustain 200 DPS enemy

**Increased Attack Speed** (offense scaling)
- Added to: attack skills
- Bonus: +10% attack speed per level
- Gem level 20: +200% attack speed total
- Synergy: compounds with weapon attack speed
- Use Case: enables 2.5+ attacks per second (endgame DPS)

**Increased Mana Efficiency** (resource scaling)
- Added to: spell skills
- Bonus: -20% mana cost of linked gem
- Scaling: reduces mana requirement, enables more casts
- Use: prevents mana starvation in long fights

1. Earthquake (primary skill)

- Added Fire: 1,508 + 1,000 fire = 2,508 total

**6-Socket Body Armor Setup**:
1. Incinerate (primary spell)
2. Spell Echo (1.6x damage)
3. Added Cold Damage (elemental hybrid)
4. Increased Area of Effect (+area)
5. Increased Cast Speed (utility)
6. Increased Mana Efficiency (resource)

**DPS Calculation**:
- Base: 400 fire damage (gem level 20, stage 5 damage)
- Spell Echo: 400 × 1.6 = 640 per cast-pair
- Added Cold: 640 + 300 = 940 total

- DPS: 1,880 / 0.25 = 7,520 DPS

**Secondary Setup** (3-Socket Helm):
1. Frostbolt (utility spell)
2. Faster Cast (+cast speed)
3. Mana Leech

### 3.3 Poison Assassin Build

**6-Socket Setup**:
1. Reave (primary attack)
2. Poison (converts damage to poison)
3. Multistrike (2.32x multiplier)
4. Increased Attack Speed (enable stack application)
5. Added Chaos Damage (pure poison damage)
6. Increased Mana Efficiency

**Poison Mechanics**:
- Base attack damage: 500
- Poison application: each hit applies poison
- Poison DPS: 500 × 0.20 = 100 per stack
- Stacking: 8 simultaneous stacks (Crimson Dance passive)
- Total poison DPS: 800 DPS (100 × 8)

**Attack Speed Requirement**:
- Need 4 attacks/second to apply 8 poison stacks in 2 seconds
- Base attack: 1.0 APS
- Multistrike: adds 2 repeats per attack = 3.0 APS
- Increased Attack Speed support: +100% = 6.0 APS total

**Final DPS**: 6 attacks/second × 2.32 multiplier × 500 damage × (100 poison DPS per stack × 8 stacks) = 50,000+ DPS

## Part 4: Gem Leveling and Corruption

### 4.1 Gem Experience

Skill gems gain experience as player kills enemies. Experience scales:
- Gem XP: 10% of player kill XP
- Gem level: 1-20 base, 1-21 corrupted
- Each level: +5% damage, -2% cooldown

**Example Progression**:
- Level 1 Incinerate: 100 DPS base
- Level 10 Incinerate: 100 × 1.5 = 150 DPS (+50%)
- Level 20 Incinerate: 100 × 2.0 = 200 DPS (+100%)
- Level 21 (corrupted): 100 × 2.05 = 205 DPS (+105%)

### 4.2 Corruption Mechanic

Corrupting a gem:
- Uses: 1 Vaal Orb (currency)
- Result: gem level +1 (20 → 21)
- Risk: gem may be destroyed (30% chance)
- Benefit: +5% damage for expensive corruption

**Corruption Economy**:
- Level 20 gem value: 10 chaos
- Level 21 gem value: 50 chaos (5x premium)
- Expected value of corruption: 50 × 0.7 = 35 chaos (accounting for destruction)
- Cost: 1 Vaal Orb = 1 chaos
- Profit: 35 - 10 - 1 = 24 chaos per successful corruption

**Weapon Socket** (2-3 sockets):
- Primary skill (Earthquake, Incinerate)
- Support: Attack/Cast speed
- Support: Utility (Leech, Fortify)

**Body Armor Socket** (6 sockets):
- Primary skill (with all supports)
- Total: 1 active + 5 support gems

**Helmet Socket** (3-4 sockets):
- Utility spell (Frostbolt, Aura)
- Aura gem (Precision, Discipline)
- Utility support

**Gloves Socket** (3-4 sockets):
- Secondary attack (Whirling Blades)
- Cast-on-crit spell (if applicable)
- Support gems

**Boots Socket** (3-4 sockets):
- Movement skill (Dash, Teleport)
- Support: Faster Casts
- Utility gem

**Total Gems**: 20+ active + support combinations

### 5.2 Gem Pricing

**Gem Value Scaling**:
- Common gems (Fireball, Earthquake): 1 chaos (baseline)
- Uncommon gems (Vaal skills): 5-10 chaos
- Rare gems (unique-like): 50-100 chaos
- Level 21 gems: 30-100 chaos (corruption premium)
- Perfect gems (20/23 quality): 150+ chaos

**Quality Scaling**:
- +1% per quality point (0-20 scale)
- Quality 20 gem: +20% damage (valuable)
- Corrupting for quality: unpredictable risk

## Part 6: Gem Trading and Economy

### 6.1 Gem Flipping

Strategy for profit:
1. Buy level 1 gems from vendors (cheap, ~1 alteration shard each)
2. Level gems to 20 (requires 3-4 week farming)
3. Sell level 20 gems (10 chaos each)
4. Profit: 10 chaos per gem (extreme time investment, not recommended)

**Alternative Strategy**:
1. Farm for chaos
2. Buy level 20 gems directly from players
3. Corrupt gems to level 21 (30% success rate)
4. Sell level 21 gems for 50+ chaos
5. Profit: 35-40 chaos per corruption (better ROI)

### 6.2 Meta Gem Shifts

**Current Meta** (season 24):
- Incinerate: 50 chaos (hot skill)
- Spell Echo: 10 chaos (always relevant)
- Multistrike: 20 chaos (physical heavy)
- Frenzy: 30 chaos (berserker staple)

**Previous Meta** (season 23):
- Earthquake: 100 chaos (was "top" skill)
- Vaal Earthquake: 200 chaos (now nerfed)
- Spell Echo: 5 chaos (still relevant)

Meta shifts occur when developers balance patch skills (nerf strong, buff weak).
