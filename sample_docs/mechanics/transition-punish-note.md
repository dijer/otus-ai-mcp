# Path of Exile 2: Transition Punish Note

A transition is not a damage window until the boss has actually committed to the next state.

If the arena changes, reset your position.
If the boss is invisible, untargetable, or mid-script, do not spend burst.
If the boss is clearly recovering, punish with your strongest safe rotation.

The safer choice is usually to wait half a second longer than to lose a full phase of cooldowns.
