# Path of Exile 2: Boss Phase Transition Notes

## Part 1: Transition Meaning

A phase transition is the moment the boss changes behavior, layout, or target rules. During transitions, damage may be reduced, blocked, or wasted.

## Part 2: Signals

Common signals of transition:
- Boss pauses briefly.
- Boss moves to center.
- Arena changes color.
- Voice line triggers.
- Adds spawn.

## Part 3: Player Response

Correct response:
- Stop greedy damage.
- Reposition first.
- Wait for the next safe opening.
- Refresh defenses if needed.

Incorrect response:
- Use all burst while the boss is untargetable.
- Stand still and hope the phase ends safely.
- Assume the pattern will stay the same.

## Part 4: Transition Safety Rule

If the boss has changed state, re-evaluate the fight. Do not keep following the old pattern.

## Conclusion

Transitions are where many boss deaths happen. The safest play is to treat every phase change as a short reset of the encounter rhythm.
