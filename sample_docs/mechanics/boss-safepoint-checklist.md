# Path of Exile 2: Boss Safepoint Checklist

## Part 1: Before Entry

Check these before entering a boss arena:
- Flasks full.
- Movement skill ready.
- Main burst cooldown ready.
- Defensive buff active.
- Boss mechanic understood.

## Part 2: During Fight

Use this loop:
1. Watch the boss animation.
2. Move first if the attack is not yet committed.
3. Damage only after the boss has clearly opened a window.
4. Stop damage if the next mechanic is about to begin.

## Part 3: Safe Points

A safepoint is a moment where damage is lower than movement risk.

Typical safepoints:
- After a missed leap.
- After a channel ends.
- After a summon cycle.
- After a forced reposition.

## Part 4: Unsafe Points

Unsafe points are moments where greed kills runs.

Typical unsafe points:
- Boss is winding up a slam.
- Arena is filling with hazards.
- Transition is about to begin.
- Boss is targeting the player with a follow-up chain.

## Conclusion

Boss kills improve when players stop guessing and start reading safepoints. Damage is best spent when the boss cannot punish movement.
