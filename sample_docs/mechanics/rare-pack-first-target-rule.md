# Path of Exile 2: Rare Pack First Target Rule

When a rare pack appears, choose the first target by threat, not by health.

## Order

1. Monster that can kill you fastest.
2. Monster that moves fastest.
3. Monster that creates hazards or summons.
4. Monster that only looks tanky.

## Simple Rule

If two monsters seem equally dangerous, kill the one that reduces your movement options.

## End Rule

The pack that gives you room to move is safer than the pack that only gives you room to think.
