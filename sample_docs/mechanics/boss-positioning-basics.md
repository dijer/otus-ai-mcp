# Path of Exile 2: Boss Positioning Basics

## Core Rule

Stand where the boss can be punished but cannot easily kill you.

## Simple Positions

- Front: dangerous against slams.
- Side: usually safer for melee bosses.
- Back: good if the boss turns slowly.
- Edge of arena: best for kiting and escape.

## Positioning Rule

If the boss is about to move, move first.
If the boss is about to attack, do not stand still.

## Conclusion

Good positioning turns a hard boss into a readable pattern. Bad positioning turns a readable pattern into a death.
