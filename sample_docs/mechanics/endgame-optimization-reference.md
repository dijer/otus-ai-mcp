## Part 3: Survival in Endgame

### 3.1 Death Prevention Checklist

**Pre-Map Checklist** (before entering tier 16):
- [ ] Life pool: 3,500+ (verify in character screen)
- [ ] Resistances: 75% all confirmed (check status bar)
- [ ] Flasks: full (all 5 flasks charged)
- [ ] Buffs: auras active (Precision, Discipline, etc.)
- [ ] Mechanics: understand boss fight before entering

**During Map Checklist**:
- [ ] Avoid ground effects (walk around poison clouds)
- [ ] Maintain distance from rare packs (kiting distance)
- [ ] Watch boss positioning (anticipate next attack)
- [ ] Manage flask charges (use before critical)
- [ ] Monitor life (keep above 60% at all times)

**Death-Inducing Situations**:
1. Underestimating rare pack damage
2. Standing in ground effects
3. Running out of flasks (becoming vulnerable)
4. Ignoring stagger buildup
5. Aggressive play against unfamiliar boss

### 3.2 Danger Assessment

**Low Danger** (green light):
- Rare pack: 2 rares
- Damage: standard mods
- Life: >80%
- Action: aggressive attacking

**Medium Danger** (yellow light):
- Rare pack: 3-4 rares
- Damage: +25% mods
- Life: 60-80%
- Action: cautious, maintain distance

**High Danger** (red light):
- Rare pack: 5+ rares OR boss
- Damage: +50% or debuff mods
- Life: <60%
- Action: retreat, flask up, reset

**Extreme Danger** (flee):
- Stagger locked by multiple rares
- HP <30% and no flasks
- Boss in aggressive phase
- Action: panic flask, run to safe zone, reset

### 3.3 Hardcore Survival Strategy

**Tier 11-14 Progression**:
- Run cautiously: 8-10 minute clears (safety priority)
- Skip dangerous mods: "curse effectiveness", "no healing"
- Maintain 3+ deaths maximum per session (acceptable failure rate)
- If >1 death per 3 maps: STOP, gear up more

**Tier 15-16 Progression**:
- Extreme caution: 10-15 minute clears (ultra-conservative)
- Skip 50% of mods that seem risky
- Accept 1 death per 5 maps as normal (20% death rate)
- Build extremely defensive (sacrifice 20% DPS for 50% defense)
