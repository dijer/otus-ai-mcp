# Path of Exile 2: Passive Skill Tree Complete Reference

## Overview

The passive skill tree in Path of Exile 2 is a large interconnected web of nodes that modify character stats. Characters start at different positions on the tree based on their class. The tree contains approximately 1,500 notable nodes and 3,200 small nodes.

---

## Tree Structure

### Node Types

#### Small Nodes
- Provide minor stat bonuses (e.g., +10 life, +5% increased damage).
- Required to traverse to more valuable nodes.
- Example: "+12 to Maximum Life", "5% increased Physical Damage".

#### Notable Nodes
- Provide meaningful bonuses.
- Located at intersections of travel paths.
- Examples: "Elemental Overload", "Iron Reflexes", "Acrobatics".

#### Keystone Nodes
- Provide powerful bonuses that fundamentally alter mechanics.
- Always come with significant trade-offs.
- Located at the outer edges of the tree or in isolated clusters.
- Examples: "Chaos Inoculation", "Resolute Technique", "Vaal Pact".

#### Mastery Nodes
- Unlocked by allocating nearby notable nodes.
- Allow selection of a bonus from a thematic pool.
- One mastery per notable cluster.

---

## Notable Keystones

### Chaos Inoculation
- Effect: Life is set to 1; maximum Energy Shield is increased by 50%.
- Trade-off: Any life damage is lethal; build must rely entirely on Energy Shield.
- Best for: Witch Occultist, high-ES investment builds.
- Counter-interaction: Cannot use life flasks.
- Verification Fact: Chaos Inoculation sets life to exactly 1.

### Resolute Technique
- Effect: Never deal critical strikes; never miss attacks.
- Trade-off: All critical strike investment is wasted.
- Best for: Builds that invest in attack speed rather than crit.
- Counter-interaction: Cannot activate on-crit effects.
- Verification Fact: Resolute Technique makes all attacks 100% hit chance.

### Vaal Pact
- Effect: Life regeneration has no effect; life leech applies instantly.
- Trade-off: Regeneration builds are incompatible.
- Best for: Berserker or builds with very high life leech.
- Counter-interaction: Cannot use regeneration-based flasks.

### Iron Reflexes
- Effect: Converts all evasion rating to armor.
- Trade-off: Cannot evade attacks at all.
- Best for: Hybrid armor-evasion base builds wanting pure armor.
- Note: Highly effective when combined with Jade Flask.
- Verification Fact: Iron Reflexes converts 100% of evasion to armor.

### Acrobatics
- Effect: 30% chance to dodge attacks; 50% less armor and energy shield.
- Trade-off: Significant reduction in mitigation stats.
- Best for: Ranger Pathfinder or Duelist Gladiator with evasion investment.
- Verification Fact: Acrobatics provides exactly 30% dodge chance with 50% armor reduction.

### Elemental Overload
- Effect: If you have dealt a critical strike in the last 8 seconds, spells deal 40% more elemental damage.
- Trade-off: Requires at least one crit ability in the build.
- Best for: Templar Inquisitor or hybrid caster builds.
- Verification Fact: Elemental Overload requires a critical strike within exactly 8 seconds.

## Notable Passive Clusters by Theme

### Life Cluster (Central Tree)
Key Notables:
- Thick Skin: +40 life, 12% increased maximum life.
- Golem's Blood: +50 life, 1.2% life regeneration per second.
- Sanguine Path: +30 life, 15% increased life recovery from flasks.
- Vitality Void: +40 life, 10% of damage leeched as life.

Optimal pathing: characters starting in the Strength region (Warrior, Duelist, Templar) can access these with 4-6 travel nodes.

---

### Evasion Cluster (Lower Right Tree)
Key Notables:
- Fleet Footed: 15% increased movement speed, 20% increased evasion.
- Phase Acrobatics: 10% chance to dodge spell hits.
- Blur: Nearby enemies have 5% reduced chance to hit.
- Evasive Instinct: After dodging, gain 30% increased evasion for 1 second.

Optimal pathing: Ranger and Shadow classes start adjacent to this cluster.

---

### Energy Shield Cluster (Upper Right Tree)
Key Notables:
- Arcane Focus: 25% increased energy shield, 10% reduced mana cost.
- Sanctity: 15% more energy shield recharge rate.
- Mind Over Matter: 30% of damage taken from mana before life.
- Clarity of Purpose: 20% increased mana recovery, 5% increased spell damage.

Optimal pathing: Witch starts at the top of the tree near these clusters.

---

### Critical Strike Cluster (Multiple locations)
Key Notables:
- Deadly Repartee: 35% increased critical strike chance with swords.
- Precise Technique: 40% more critical strike multiplier when life is full.
- Blood Drinker: 0.8% of attack damage leeched as life on critical strike.
- Razor's Edge: Your critical strikes ignore 25% of enemy armor.

Note: Razor's Edge is particularly effective against the Ironclad Ravager whose high armor reduces physical damage significantly.

---

### Mana Cluster (Left Tree)
Key Notables:
- Mana Flows: 30% increased mana, 20% reduced mana cost.
- Arcane Blessing: Arcane Surge applies when you spend 100 mana.
- Eldritch Battery: Spend energy shield instead of mana for skills.
- Meditation: 1% mana regeneration per second.

## Class Starting Positions and Travel Paths

### Warrior (Top Left)
Nearest clusters:
- Life clusters (3-5 nodes)
- Armor clusters (2-4 nodes)
- Melee damage clusters (4-6 nodes)
Travel cost to evasion clusters: 18-22 nodes (inefficient).

### Ranger (Bottom Right)
Nearest clusters:
- Evasion clusters (2-4 nodes)
- Projectile damage clusters (3-5 nodes)
- Flask clusters (6-8 nodes)
Travel cost to life clusters: 15-20 nodes (moderate).

### Witch (Top Right)
Nearest clusters:
- Energy shield clusters (2-4 nodes)
- Spell damage clusters (3-5 nodes)
- Minion clusters (5-7 nodes)
Travel cost to armor clusters: 20-25 nodes (very inefficient).

### Duelist (Bottom Left)
Nearest clusters:
- Melee damage clusters (3-5 nodes)
- Bleed clusters (4-6 nodes)
- Critical strike clusters (5-8 nodes)
Travel cost to spell clusters: 16-22 nodes (moderate).

### Shadow (Bottom Right of Center)
Nearest clusters:
- Critical strike clusters (2-4 nodes)
- Poison clusters (3-5 nodes)
- Trap and mine clusters (4-6 nodes)
Travel cost to life clusters: 12-16 nodes (reasonable).

### Templar (Top Center)
Nearest clusters:
- Aura clusters (2-4 nodes)
- Elemental damage clusters (3-6 nodes)
- Life clusters (5-8 nodes)
Travel cost to evasion clusters: 22-28 nodes (very inefficient).

## Ascendancy Passive Trees

Each Ascendancy has 8 additional nodes unique to that specialization.

### Berserker Ascendancy Tree (Warrior)
- Rage: Gain rage stacks on hit; each stack provides 1% increased attack damage.
- War Bringer: Warcries cost no mana and grant rage.
- Rite of Ruin: Maximum rage increased by 50; losing rage deals damage equal to 1% of rage lost.
- Unflinching: Gain 1% of maximum life as flat life regeneration per rage stack.
- Pain Reaver: Recover 1.5% of maximum life per second for each second you have been hit recently.
- Crave the Slaughter: Attack speed is 10% increased for each rage stack above 20.
- Aspect of Carnage: Take 10% more damage; deal 40% more damage.
- Berserk (Active Skill): Consumes all rage to boost damage proportional to rage consumed.

### Deadeye Ascendancy Tree (Ranger)
- Gathering Winds: Tailwind effect on kill; each stack increases projectile speed.
- Rupture: Attacks have 10% chance to cause bleeding; bleed deals 30% more damage.
- Eagle Eye: 15% reduced projectile spread; projectiles chain an additional time.
- Focal Point: Your marks have 25% increased effect.
- Ricochet: Projectiles can chain to up to 5 additional targets.
- Far Shot: Projectiles deal 20% more damage at maximum range.
- Endless Munitions: +2 projectiles.
- Wind Ward: Tailwind grants dodge chance equal to number of tailwind stacks.

### Necromancer Ascendancy Tree (Witch)
- Corpse Pact: Each corpse consumed by your skills grants 1% increased cast speed for 4 seconds.
- Mistress of Sacrifice: 20% of life and mana costs of skills are paid by consuming corpses.
- Commander of Darkness: Auras affecting minions also affect you.
- Plaguebringer: Corpses you create explode on death.
- Golem Commander: Can summon up to 2 additional golems.
- Bone Sculptor: Raised skeletons have 50% more maximum life.
- Flesh Sculptor: Raised Zombies have 30% more life and deal 30% more damage.
- Minion Instability: Minions explode at 1 HP dealing 33% of max life as fire damage.

## Passive Tree Efficiency Tips

### Travel Node Cost Reduction
- Some unique jewels reduce the travel node cost between clusters.
- Thread of Hope: Allocate notables in a radius without pathing to them (3-jewel cost, saves 5-12 nodes).

### Cluster Jewels (Endgame)
- Can be socketed in the outer tree rings.
- Add 2-3 additional notables to any cluster.
- Fundamental endgame tree optimization tool.
- Large Cluster Jewel: 12 nodes, 2 notables minimum.
- Medium Cluster Jewel: 8 nodes, 1 notable minimum.
- Small Cluster Jewel: 4 nodes, 1 notable minimum.

### Point Budget Guidelines
- Act 1-6 completion grants approximately 90 passive points.
- Endgame completion and Labyrinth grants 4 additional Ascendancy points.
- Total available at endgame: approximately 123 passive + 8 Ascendancy.

## Verification Facts
- Chaos Inoculation sets life to exactly 1 HP.
- Acrobatics provides exactly 30% dodge chance and reduces armor/energy shield by exactly 50%.
- Elemental Overload requires a critical strike within exactly 8 seconds.
- Iron Reflexes converts 100% of evasion to armor with no evasion retained.
- Berserker maximum rage can be increased to 50 above base with Rite of Ruin.
- Total passive points available at endgame is approximately 123 skill points plus 8 ascendancy points.
