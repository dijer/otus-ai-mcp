# Path of Exile 2: Itemization, Gear Progression, and Unique Items

## Part 1: Item Rarity and Base Types

### 1.1 Item Rarity Tiers

**White (Normal) Items**:
- No mods, only base stats
- Value: vendor 5 for 1 alchemy shard
- Uses: item bases for crafting when rare-quality bases

**Blue (Magic) Items**:
- 1-2 mods (prefix + suffix typically)
- Tier: 2-3x vendor value of white
- Uses: early-game equipping, vendor for transmutation shards

**Rare Items**:
- 3-6 mods (combination of prefixes and suffixes)
- Value: highly variable (1-1,000+ chaos depending on roll quality)
- Uses: primary endgame equipping, crafting investment base

**Unique Items**:
- Fixed mods and explicit names
- Value: 1-10,000+ chaos depending on build relevance
- Uses: build-enabling items, rare drops, targeted farming

### 1.2 Item Bases and Their Roles

**Body Armor Bases**:
- Plate Armor: 500+ armor (physical defense)
- Astral Plate: 400 armor + 100 ES (hybrid defense)
- Vaal Regalia: 200 ES (pure energy shield)
- Leather Garb: 200 armor + evasion (hybrid evasion)

Picking the right base determines the defense archetype:
- Plate → armor-based builds (Berserker, Gladiator)
- Astral → hybrid builds (Templar, Witch)
- Vaal Regalia → spell builds (Sorcerer, Witch)
- Leather → evasion builds (Ranger, Shadow)

**Weapon Bases**:
- 1H Sword: 150-200 pDPS, scales with attack speed
- 2H Sword: 250-350 pDPS, lower attack speed (1.2x)
- Wand: 80-120 pDPS + spell scaling, enables dual-wielding spells
- Staff: 200+ pDPS + spell damage, 2-hand requirement

Weapon base choice determines skill availability and scaling path.

### 1.3 Crafting Fundamentals

**Crafting Process**:
1. Start with white base item (buy for 1-10 chaos depending on tier)
2. Use Orb of Transmutation (0.04 chaos) → blue item
3. Use Orb of Alteration (0.02 chaos) → reroll mods repeatedly
4. When satisfied with mods, use Orb of Augmentation (0.1 chaos) → add extra mod if not full
5. Final step: use Orb of Regal (5-8 chaos) → lock in final mods, item becomes rare

**Total crafting cost**: ~50 chaos per item for basic rare crafting.

**Advanced crafting**:
- Use Exalted Orb (150 chaos) to add new mod to existing rare
- Use Annulment Orb (60 chaos) to remove unwanted mod
- Use Divine Orb (120 chaos) to reroll mod values within valid ranges

## Part 2: Unique Items and Build Enablers

### 2.1 Signature Unique Items by Archetype

**Earthquake Berserker Enablers**:
- Kaom's Heart (500 flat life unique body): Mandatory for pure phys builds, eliminates socket dependency
- Belly of the Beast (400 life unique body): Alternative, allows gem socket usage
- Marohi Erqi (300+ pDPS unique 2H mace): Defines the earthquake weapon of choice

**Incinerate Templar Enablers**:
- Crown of the Inquisitor (spell level +1 unique helm): Grants +1 spell level (30% damage multiplier)
- Kaom's Roots (unique boots, no flinch): Mandatory for spell survivability
- The Fulcrum (unique staff, spell damage focus): Alternative 2-hand scaling

**Cyclone Warrior Enablers**:
- Kaom's Primacy (unique 1H sword, life + phys): Attack speed enabler
- Daresso's Salute (unique boots, frenzy generation): Mandatory for attack speed scaling
- Atziri's Disfavor (unique 2H poleaxe): Alternative endgame weapon

**Mind Over Matter Hierophant Enablers**:
- Tukohama's Fortress (unique shield, +150 mana): Mandatory for mana scaling
- Astramentis (unique amulet, +40 all attributes): Balances gear requirements
- Vis Mortis (unique body armor, +200 mana + ES hybrid): Advanced scaling gear

### 2.2 Universal Utility Uniques

**Mandatory for All Builds**:
- Kaom's Roots (unique boots): Prevents flinch (loss of action)
- Atziri's Promise (flask, 5-10 chaos): Chaos damage + leech scaling
- Taste of Hate (flask, 50+ chaos): Cold damage + leech scaling
- The wise oak (flask, 10+ chaos): Resist pen scaling (all types)

**Situational Uniques**:
- Acuity gloves (unique, 150+ chaos): Grants crit-on-crit healing
- Shaper's Touch (unique gloves): ES scaling from precision
- Lycosidae (unique shield, 20 chaos): Guaranteed accuracy (enables crit builds)

### 2.3 Economy of Unique Drop Rates

Unique drop rates scale with rarity and tier:

**Common Uniques** (drop rates 0.5-1%):
- Kaom's Roots: 10-20 chaos
- Belly of the Beast: 15-25 chaos
- Atziri's Promise: 5-10 chaos

**Rare Uniques** (drop rates 0.01-0.1%):
- Kaom's Heart: 200-500 chaos (early league)
- Acuity gloves: 500-2,000 chaos
- Mirror of Kalandra: not purchasable (cosmetic only)

**Farming vs. Buying**:
- Common uniques: farm yourself (save 30-50% cost)
- Rare uniques: buy directly (farming drop rate too low, 50+ hours expected)
- Endgame-critical uniques: buy on day 1 (price peak), resell later

## Part 3: Gear Progression Path

### 3.1 Early Game (Acts 1-2) Gear Requirements

**Level 1-10 Gear**:
- Life requirement: 100+ per item
- Resistance: not required, resistances cap at level 11
- Damage: any weapon with 50+ DPS viable

**Level 11-25 Gear** (after act 1):
- Life requirement: 300+ per item
- Resistance: allocate +30 all (75% cap pending)
- Damage: 100+ DPS weapons required for act 2 bosses

**Sourcing early gear**:
- Quest rewards (provide 30% of needed gear)
- Early rare drops (blues from mobs, rares from rares)
- Vendor progression (alchemy→rare conversion)

### 3.2 Mid Game (Acts 3-5) Gear Requirements

**Level 26-50 Gear** (act 3-4):
- Life requirement: 500-800 per item
- Resistance: maintain 75% cap on all types
- Damage: 300+ weapon DPS for act 3 bosses, 500+ for act 4

**Level 51+ Gear** (act 5-6):
- Life requirement: 800-1,200 per item
- Resistance: 75% all + chaos resistance 20%+
- Damage: 700+ weapon DPS for act 5 bosses, 900+ for act 6

**Sourcing mid-game gear**:
- Crafted rares from bases (50-200 chaos per item)
- Unique drops (enable specific builds, non-negotiable)
- Trading (buy items off other players in same league)

### 3.3 Endgame (Act 6+) Gear Requirements

**Tier 1-5 Maps** (level 68-72):
- Life requirement: 1,500-2,000
- Resistance: 75% all + 30%+ chaos
- Damage: 1,000+ DPS minimum

**Tier 6-10 Maps** (level 73-76):
- Life requirement: 2,000-2,500
- Resistance: 75% all + 50%+ chaos
- Damage: 1,500+ DPS minimum

**Tier 11-16 Maps** (level 77-83):
- Life requirement: 2,500-3,500+
- Resistance: 75% all + 75%+ chaos + overcap 10% for -resist maps
- Damage: 2,000+ DPS minimum

**Gear budget for full endgame setup**:
- Body armor: 200-500 chaos
- Weapon(s): 100-300 chaos
- Helm: 50-150 chaos
- Boots: 50-100 chaos
- Rings: 50-200 chaos each
- Amulet: 50-200 chaos
- Total: 500-1,650 chaos = 3-10 exalted orbs

This represents 3-8 hours of farming with established builds.

## Part 4: Modifier Priority and Itemization Strategy

### 4.1 Mandatory Modifiers by Slot

**Body Armor**:
- Mandatory: +100+ life
- Priority 1: +20% elemental resistances
- Priority 2: +10% damage (archetype-specific)
- Priority 3: +10% armor or evasion

**Boots**:
- Mandatory: +25% movement speed
- Priority 1: +60+ life
- Priority 2: +20% resistance or 10% phase dodge

**Rings**:
- Mandatory: +life + resistances
- Priority 1: +damage (phys, ele, or spell)
- Priority 2: +attack speed or cast speed

**Amulet**:
- Mandatory: +life
- Priority 1: +all attributes (balances gear)
- Priority 2: +damage scaling

### 4.2 Conditional Modifiers (Build-Specific)

**For Physical Builds**:
- Accuracy: +200 minimum (caps hit chance at 95%)
- Armor: +50% effective defense (stacks with resistances)
- Life leech: +2-3% for sustain
- Weapon damage: increased physical/added flat

**For Spell Builds**:
- Cast speed: +10% per item (stacks for 6 items = 60% total)
- Spell damage: +30% per item (stacks for 6 items = 180% total)
- Mana: +100-200 per item for mana sustain
- Energy shield: +100+ on body and helm

**For Hybrid Builds**:
- Mixed damage: +phys +elemental to scale both
- Mixed defense: +armor +ES to hybrid tank
- Dual scaling: keystones enable double damage type

### 4.3 Trading Strategy for Optimal Gear

**Optimal trading sequence**:
1. Farm 1,000 chaos worth of gear (maps + uniques)
2. Identify 5 gear slots needing upgrade
3. Target <300 chaos budget per slot (don't overspend on single items)
4. Buy 5 items simultaneously (get 5-10% bulk discount)
5. Back to farming with upgraded gear
6. Repeat every 100 chaos farmed

This strategy keeps gear progression consistent with farming progress.

## Part 5: Item Filters and Loot Optimization

### 5.1 Loot Filter Purpose

Loot filters hide unnecessary items on screen, displaying only high-value items. This increases farming speed by 20-30% (less time reading item names, more time killing).

**Hidden item categories**:
- Normal (white) bases except specific high-tier bases
- Blue items (magic) except resistance bases or jewelry
- Low-tier unique items (value <10 chaos)
- Scrolls and shards (hundreds drop, vendor worthless)

**Displayed item categories**:
- Rare items (all colors)
- Unique items (all colors)
- High-tier crafting bases (certain whites that are valuable)
- Currency and divination cards
- Maps tier 8+

### 5.2 Filter Strictness and Economy

Different filter strictness for different contexts:

**Very Strict** (endgame tier 15-16):
- Display: uniques + rare drops only
- Hide: all whites, all blues, low-rarity items
- Speed: maximum (less processing, faster killing)

**Strict** (endgame tier 11-14):
- Display: uniques + rares + some bases
- Hide: whites below tier 10, all blues
- Speed: fast (balanced display)

**Normal** (maps tier 6-10):
- Display: all uniques + rares + many bases
- Hide: only normal/magic item spam
- Speed: moderate (learning good items)

**Very Liberal** (acts and early maps):
- Display: all items except vendor trash
- Hide: vendor trash (scrolls, shards, wisdom)
- Speed: slowest (find all valuable items while learning)

## Conclusion

Itemization is the core endgame progression loop. Optimal strategy:
1. Establish base damage and survivability in acts
2. Transition to rare crafting in maps 1-5 (budget gear, high volume)
3. Target specific uniques in maps 6-10 (build-enabling items)
4. Endgame optimize in maps 11-16 (perfect rolls, high budgets)

Wealth accumulation through itemization farming enables all other endgame activities (bossing, challenges, PvP).
