# Path of Exile 2: Monster Types, Rare Affixes, and Map Modifiers

## Part 1: Monster Classification

### 1.1 Monster Categories

**Normal Monsters**:
- Health: 1x baseline
- Damage: 1x baseline
- Drops: common items only (whites, blues)
- Examples: zombie, skeleton, cultist
- Threat Level: low (solo killable)

**Magic Monsters** (blue mobs):
- Health: 2x baseline (4 HP)
- Damage: 1.25x baseline
- Affixes: 1-2 additional mods (see section 1.3)
- Drops: blue items + currency
- Threat Level: medium (kill quickly)

**Rare Monsters** (yellow mobs):
- Health: 4x baseline (8 HP)
- Damage: 1.5x baseline
- Affixes: 2-4 additional mods
- Drops: rare items + currency + unique chance (1%)
- Threat Level: high (requires focus)

**Unique Monsters** (orange/gold):
- Health: 6-8x baseline
- Damage: 2-3x baseline
- Affixes: 4+ mods + unique mechanics
- Drops: guaranteed rare items + high currency
- Threat Level: extreme (potential bosses)

**Boss Monsters**:
- Health: 10-50x baseline (depends on act)
- Damage: 3-5x baseline
- Special mechanics: phase transitions, add spawning
- Drops: guaranteed unique + massive currency
- Threat Level: critical (build-defining fights)

### 1.2 Monster Mods (Affixes)

**Offensive Mods**:
- "Increased Damage" (+20% damage per mod)
- "Elemental Damage" (+50% fire/cold/lightning)
- "Critical Strikes" (+50% crit chance)
- "Life Leech" (monsters heal 2% per hit)
- "Haste" (+50% attack speed)

**Defensive Mods**:
- "Increased Health" (+50% HP per mod)
- "Regeneration" (+100 life/second)
- "Resistance" (+20% all resistances)
- "Spell Immunity" (specific element immunity)
- "Damage Avoidance" (50% damage reduction)

**Utility Mods**:
- "Summons Adds" (spawns minions on kill)
- "Converts Damage" (physical to elemental)
- "Aura Buff" (applies buff to nearby monsters)
- "Temporal Distortion" (slows player actions)

**Penalty for Players**:
- Each mod adds 5-10% difficulty
- 2 mods: +15-20% difficulty (manageable)
- 3 mods: +30% difficulty (challenging)
- 4+ mods: +50%+ difficulty (extreme)

### 1.3 Rare Monster Combinations

**Example Rare Combo 1**: "Haste + Spell Immunity + Critical Strikes"
- Attack frequency: +50% (haste)
- Damage type: spell immunity prevents one damage type
- Crit rate: +50%
- Total threat: high (aggressive, specialized counter needed)

**Example Rare Combo 2**: "Increased Health + Regeneration + Life Leech"
- Durability: +50% + 100 hp/sec regen = 6x harder to kill
- Healing: monsters heal 2% per hit
- Total threat: extreme (requires burst damage)

**Strategy for Dangerous Rares**:
1. Kite at distance (prevent hits = prevent healing)
2. Use burst damage (overcome regeneration)
3. Identify weakness (spell immunity? avoid spells, use phys)
4. Ignore if not threatening (skip and farm efficient rares)

## Part 2: Map Modifiers

### 2.1 Map Mod Categories

**Damage Mods** (increase monster damage):
- "Monsters deal +25% physical damage"
- "Monsters have +50% elemental damage"
- "Rare and unique monsters are critical strikes"
- Impact: enemies harder, your defenses tested
- Avoidance: don't run this mod if unprepared

**Resistance Mods** (enemy resistance):
- "Monsters have +20% all resistances"
- "Monsters have +30% chaos resistance"
- Impact: penetration needed to overcome
- Avoidance: converts DPS (physical to elemental if weak to phys penetration)

**Unique Mods** (mechanic changes):
- "Unique monsters have 50% increased rarity"
- "Rare monsters drop additional items"
- "Magic monsters drop additional rare items"
- Impact: loot quality increases
- Benefit: higher profit (ignore downsides for loot)

**Debuff Mods** (apply to player):
- "Players are cursed with vulnerability"
- "Players are cursed with weakness"
- "Players are cursed with warlords mark"
- Impact: enemy damage +30%, player defense -20%
- Mitigation: curse immunity gear/passives

**Environmental Mods**:
- "Area contains ground effects" (poison clouds)
- "Area has reduced visibility" (fog, harder to see)
- "Area has monster movement speed +" (enemies faster)
- Impact: movement-dependent difficulty

### 2.2 Dangerous Mod Combinations

**Deadly Combo 1**: "Curse Immunity + Curse Effectiveness + Damage"
- Enemies can't be cursed (can't debuff them)
- Curses on you are 2x stronger (defensive curse breaks)
- Monster damage +30%
- Solution: avoid curse scaling, use defensive passives instead

**Deadly Combo 2**: "Spell Immunity + Projectile Immunity + Increased Health"
- One damage type completely blocked
- Can't use projectile attacks
- Health x2
- Solution: use third damage type (elemental vs phys proj+spell)

**Deadly Combo 3**: "Vulnerability + Weakness + Haste"
- You take +50% more damage
- You deal -30% damage
- Enemies attack 50% faster
- Solution: skip mod (profit isn't worth risk)

### 2.3 Map Modifier Valuation

**Valuable Mods** (worth running despite difficulty):
- "Double Rarity" mods: +40% rare drops (profit ≈ 150 chaos)
- "Double Item Drops": +50% quantity (profit ≈ 100 chaos)
- "Unique Monster Rarity": +1 guaranteed unique per map (profit ≈ 50 chaos)

**Dangerous Mods** (skip if less profitable than alternative maps):
- "No Monster Healing" (if running life leech rares)
- "Cannot Dodge" (if dodge-based build)
- "Players Cannot Crit" (if crit-dependent)

**Ideal Map Mods** (always run):
- "Area contains breach"
- "Area contains essence" (farming specific essences)
- "Cannot Dodge Attacks" (if you aren't dodge-based)

## Part 3: Map Tiers and Difficulty Scaling

### 3.1 Tier Progression

**Tiers 1-3** (White Maps):
- Level: 68-70
- Monster stats: baseline low
- Completion time: 3-5 minutes
- Deaths: 0 (if adequately geared for act 6)
- Profit: 20-30 chaos per map

**Tiers 4-6** (Yellow Maps):
- Level: 71-73
- Monster stats: 1.2-1.5x scaling
- Completion time: 4-6 minutes
- Deaths: 0-1 (rare if cautious)
- Profit: 40-60 chaos per map

**Tiers 7-10** (Yellow Maps):
- Level: 74-76
- Monster stats: 1.5-2.0x scaling
- Completion time: 5-8 minutes
- Deaths: 1-2 (occasional, higher difficulty)
- Profit: 60-100 chaos per map

**Tiers 11-14** (Red Maps):
- Level: 77-79
- Monster stats: 2.0-3.0x scaling
- Completion time: 6-12 minutes
- Deaths: 1-5 (challenging, skill-dependent)
- Profit: 100-200 chaos per map

**Tiers 15-16** (Red Maps):
- Level: 80-83
- Monster stats: 3.0-5.0x scaling
- Completion time: 8-15 minutes
- Deaths: 2-10 (extreme difficulty, hardcore-risky)
- Profit: 200-500 chaos per map (requires perfect execution)

### 3.2 Difficulty Scaling by Map Tier

**Stat Scaling Per Tier**:
- Monster HP: +10% per tier
- Monster damage: +12% per tier
- Rarity: +5% per tier

**Example Scaling**:
- Tier 1 monster: 100 HP, 50 DPS
- Tier 6 monster: 100 × 1.10^5 = 161 HP, 50 × 1.12^5 = 88 DPS (+76% harder)
- Tier 16 monster: 100 × 1.10^15 = 405 HP, 50 × 1.12^15 = 261 DPS (+422% harder total)

**Defensive Requirements**:
- Tier 1: 1,500 life + 75% resistances (safe)
- Tier 6: 2,000 life + 75% resistances + 3,000 armor
- Tier 16: 3,500 life + 75% resistances + 8,000 armor + specialized mechanics

### 3.3 Map Sustain Mechanics

**Drop Rate by Source**:
- Boss drops: guaranteed 1 map per tier
- Monsters: 0.3% per monster (1 per 300 kills)
- Sextant modifiers: +1 map guaranteed per mod
- Scarabs: +2-5 maps per scarab

**Sustain Math**:
- Running tier 10 maps (clearing 200 monsters)
- Boss drop: 1 map tier 10
- Monster drops: 200 × 0.003 = 0.6 maps average
- Total sustained: 1.6 maps average per map

**Break-even Analysis**:
- Cost of map: 5 chaos (to run)
- Profit per map: 80 chaos
- Sustain: 1.6 maps drops per 1 map run
- Net profit: (1.6 × 80) - 5 = 123 chaos per original map

**Endgame Profit**: 123 chaos per 10-minute run = 738 chaos/hour

## Part 4: Rare Monster Strategies

### 4.1 Dangerous Rare Identification

**High-Threat Rares** (prioritize killing):
1. Rare + Haste + Crit Strikes = aggressive, high damage
2. Rare + Regen + Life Leech = unkillable unless burst
3. Rare + Elemental Damage + Spell Immunity = exploit weakness

**Low-Threat Rares** (can ignore):
1. Rare + Increased Health Only = slow, non-threatening
2. Rare + Curse Effects = debuff you, but weak damage
3. Rare + Summons = minions are weak, ignore

### 4.2 Rare Engagement Tactics

**Tactic 1: Kite and Burst**:
- Keep distance: stay outside melee range
- Wait for caster animation: spell casting = long window
- Burst attack: unleash all damage during window
- Re-engage: continue kiting

**Tactic 2: Stun and Lock**:
- Apply stagger: trigger poise > stun
- During stun: deal burst damage
- Avoid recovery: stun again before recovery animation

**Tactic 3: Elemental Exploitation**:
- Identify weakness: "fire immunity" → use physical
- Bypass defense: avoid their strengths
- Exploit opening: deal damage type they can't defend

## Part 5: Monster Scaling in Hardcore vs Softcore

### 5.1 Difficulty Perception

**Softcore** (non-permadeath):
- Same monsters, same stats, same difficulty
- But risk is low (don't lose permanent gear)
- Playstyle: aggressive, risk-taking allowed

**Hardcore** (permadeath):
- Same monsters, same stats, same difficulty
- But risk is extreme (lose all gear, character)
- Playstyle: conservative, risk-averse, defensive-heavy

**Effective Difficulty**:
- Hardcore: +100% subjective difficulty (same monsters, higher stakes)
- Survival requirements: 30% more life investment needed

### 5.2 Monster Behavior in Hardcore

**Monster Aggression**:
- Monsters target players with lower life first
- In hardcore: low-life characters targeted immediately
- Strategy: maintain 60-80% life minimum (avoid being targeted)

**Stagger Lock Exploitation**:
- Monsters don't execute stagger-lock strategies optimally
- But rare monsters sometimes stack attacks (accidental locks)
- Hardcore players avoid stagger entirely (buy immunity gear)

## Conclusion

Monster diversity and map modifiers create dynamic gameplay:
1. **Threat Assessment** (identify dangerous rares)
2. **Tactical Adaptation** (choose strategy per encounter)
3. **Map Selection** (choose profitable/safe combinations)
4. **Damage Optimization** (exploit weaknesses)

Mastery of monster mechanics enables efficient endgame farming and ultra-hardcore survival.
