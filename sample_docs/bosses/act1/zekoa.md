# Boss: Zekoa

## Act
- Act 1

## Role
- Chaos pressure encounter

## Core Mechanics
- Banana Reprisal: throws a banana at the player on death
- Splinter Sprint hazard trail

## Encounter Summary
- Highly mobile skirmisher with erratic pathing and trap-like lane denial.
- Encounter is simple on paper but punishes autopilot movement hard.

## Pattern Breakdown
- Splinter Sprint: rapid dash sequence leaving a temporary hazard trail.
- Feint Leap: short hop that baits defensive cooldowns.
- Peel Toss: quick projectile check used to punish straight retreat.
- Banana Reprisal (on death): delayed banana throw at player position.

## Telegraphs And Counters
- During Splinter Sprint startup, move diagonally across lanes rather than backward.
- Ignore feint hop unless real follow-up commits; keep dodge for actual strike.
- On death animation, keep moving until banana projectile resolves.

## Positioning Rules
- Fight in open center arcs, avoid narrow edge corridors.
- Keep one clean lane free of old trail hazards.
- Re-engage only after dash trail fades.

## Punish Windows
- Short punish after completed sprint chain.
- Reliable finish window after failed Peel Toss.
- Final punish is safe only if Banana Reprisal spacing is respected.

## Verification Facts
- Banana Reprisal is the canonical unique mechanic for Zekoa.
- Zekoa throws a banana at the player on death.
