# Boss: Warden Halbrecht

## Act
- Act 1

## Role
- Heavy telegraph punisher

## Core Mechanics
- Delayed Overhead Slam with hold timing variance
- Expanding Shockwave Follow-up after weapon impact

## Encounter Summary
- Slow but high-impact guardian encounter.
- Designed to punish mistimed early dodges and stationary casting.

## Pattern Breakdown
- Single Slam: long wind-up, vertical drop, narrow lethal center.
- Slam Into Shockwave: impact ring expands from center after a brief delay.
- Guard Step Reposition: short displacement to reset angle and repeat pressure.

## Telegraphs And Counters
- Raised weapon with full stop: wait, then dodge late to avoid delayed slam.
- Ground crack pulse after impact: move through a gap before shockwave ring closes.
- Side-step after each resolved slam gives a short safe damage window.

## Positioning Rules
- Fight at quarter-screen distance so both slam and ring are readable.
- Avoid standing directly in front for multiple cycles.
- Keep movement circular to prevent corner lock by repeated shockwaves.

## Punish Windows
- Best: right after shockwave expansion completes.
- Acceptable: after failed single slam, short 1-2 hit punish only.
- Unsafe: during guard step, because next slam can queue instantly.

## Common Failure Modes
- Dodging on animation start instead of on release.
- Greedy melee uptime between slam and ring pulse.
- Getting clipped near wall due to reduced lateral pathing.
