# Boss: Count Geonor

## Act
- Act 1

## Role
- Major act gatekeeper

## Encounter Summary
- Aggressive melee duelist with chained sword pressure.
- Fight rewards disciplined side-dodges over panic backpedaling.

## Key Mechanics (Quick List)
- Three-hit sword string with delayed final punish window.
- Running Sword Thrust linear engage.
- Sword Blast line projectile after charge.
- Long Swing wide arc, often chained after movement skill.
- Around 40% HP transition with shapeshift and reset tempo.

## Phase Flow
- Phase 1 (100% to about 40% HP): sword combo pressure, thrusts, and long-reach arcs.
- Transition (around 40% HP): shapeshift sequence with full heal reset.
- Phase 2: keeps core melee kit but uses faster chaining and tighter punish timing.

## Core Mechanics
- Three-hit sword string: two fast cuts into a finishing swing.
- Running Sword Thrust: linear charge that punishes greedy casting.
- Sword Blast line attack: delayed release in a straight path.
- Long Swing follow-up: wide arc after movement commitment.

## Telegraphs And Counters
- When he turns shoulders and loads the blade: prepare for combo string, disengage, then re-enter after third hit.
- When he lowers center of gravity and dashes: side-step late, not early, to dodge Running Sword Thrust.
- When blade charge glow appears: move off axis for Sword Blast, then take 1-2 quick hits.
- After a missed long arc, punish briefly; overcommitting often gets clipped by next opener.

## Positioning Rules
- Stay at diagonal mid-range to reduce linear thrust value.
- Avoid hugging arena edges, because dash plus arc can trap movement options.
- Keep one mobility charge for phase transition tempo spike.

## Common Failure Modes
- Rolling backward into Sword Blast line.
- Trying to trade during second swing of the three-hit string.
- Dumping long channels immediately after transition and getting interrupted.
