# Boss: Choir of Madness

## Act
- Act 3

## Role
- Multi-target chaos caster, sanity-pressure mechanics

## Encounter Summary
- Choir of Madness is an unusual encounter: three simultaneous choir members who alternate attacks.
- Only one choir member is vulnerable at any time (the active singer).
- The fight tests target prioritization and awareness of incoming attacks from multiple directions.

## Key Mechanics (Quick List)
- Discordant Wail: AoE sound burst from active singer, medium radius.
- Maddening Echo: delayed repeating copies of the previous ability.
- Sanity Pressure: passive stacking debuff that applies confusion visual effect.
- Choir Shift: active singer changes; old singer becomes invulnerable, new singer becomes vulnerable.
- Final Chorus (at 1 singer remaining): last singer enrages and gains all abilities simultaneously.

## Phase Flow
- Phase 1 (3 singers, full HP): standard rotation, one singer active at a time.
- Phase 2 (2 singers remain): Choir Shift frequency doubles, Maddening Echo activates.
- Phase 3 (1 singer remains): Final Chorus, all abilities available simultaneously.

## Core Mechanics

### Discordant Wail
- AoE burst centered on active singer.
- Radius is about 6 body lengths.
- Counter: stay beyond 7 body lengths from active singer while monitoring inactive ones.

### Maddening Echo
- A copy of the last ability fires again 2 seconds after original.
- Active from Phase 2 onward.
- Counter: maintain dodge readiness for 2 seconds after any ability resolves.

### Sanity Pressure
- Passively stacks while within 5 body lengths of any choir member.
- At maximum stacks (100): vision distorts for 4 seconds.
- Stacks decay when all choir members are more than 10 body lengths away.
- Counter: avoid clustering near choir members; take gaps between abilities to retreat and decay stacks.

### Choir Shift
- Active singer designation rotates every 8 seconds or when current singer takes 25% of their HP as damage.
- Brief 1.0-second window where no singer is active during transition.
- Counter: track shift timing to maximize damage windows on active singer.

### Final Chorus (Phase 3)
- Last remaining singer fires all abilities sequentially with 0.5-second gaps.
- Counter: treat as a burst phase; use all remaining mobility tools and burst DPS to finish quickly.

## Telegraphs And Counters
- Glow on singer before Discordant Wail: increase distance immediately.
- Shimmering air before Maddening Echo: maintain dodge availability.
- Red eye-glow effect on player reaching 80% Sanity Pressure: retreat and decay stacks.
- Singer rotation animation before Choir Shift: identify new active target.

## Positioning Rules
- Triangle positioning relative to three singers minimizes Sanity Pressure accumulation.
- Focus damage on one singer to trigger Phase shifts quickly.
- In Final Chorus, treat the fight as a DPS race; survivability tools should be available.

## Common Failure Modes
- Standing between two singers and accumulating Sanity Pressure from both.
- Not tracking Maddening Echo timing in Phase 2 and taking doubled hits.
- Entering Final Chorus without mobility tools available.

## Punish Windows
- After Discordant Wail: 1.6 seconds on active singer.
- After Choir Shift: 1.0 seconds while new singer is exposed but not yet attacking.
- After each Final Chorus ability: 0.5 seconds.

## Verification Facts
- Choir of Madness is an Act 3 boss in this dataset.
- Maximum Sanity Pressure stacks are 100 before vision distortion triggers.
- Choir Shift transition window is exactly 1.0 second.
- Final Chorus fires abilities with exactly 0.5-second gaps.
