# Boss: Ashen Magistrate

## Act
- Act 3

## Role
- Fire and curse hybrid caster, act 3 opener

## Encounter Summary
- Ashen Magistrate is the first major test of Act 3 and the player's first encounter with layered curse mechanics.
- He applies curses that amplify his own fire damage if the player fails to cleanse them.
- Transition at approximately 55% HP introduces dual-curse stacking.

## Key Mechanics (Quick List)
- Ember Wave: horizontal rolling fire wave, low height, must be jumped or sidestepped.
- Scorch Curse: applies burn amplification debuff for 6 seconds.
- Cinder Step: rapid teleport dash leaving fire trail.
- Magistrate's Decree: channel that applies both curses simultaneously if not interrupted by distance.
- Ashen Nova (Phase 2): large-radius fire burst at transition, unavoidable, must be survived with health buffer.

## Phase Flow
- Phase 1 (100% to about 55%): single curse application, standard fire patterns.
- Transition (around 55%): Ashen Nova fires automatically; be at full HP before this threshold.
- Phase 2: dual curses, faster Cinder Step, Magistrate's Decree channel shortens.

## Core Mechanics

### Ember Wave
- Rectangular fire wave rolls forward across arena.
- Height is low; staying stationary in a lateral position avoids it.
- Counter: sidestep rather than roll; rolls frequently clip the wave hitbox.

### Scorch Curse
- Applies Scorch debuff: player takes 25% more fire damage for 6 seconds.
- Magistrate applies this before Ember Wave sequences to amplify them.
- Counter: keep moving to reduce Scorch application frequency; natural cleanse on distance.

### Cinder Step
- Teleport dash to a position near the player.
- Leaves a fire trail for 3 seconds at departure and arrival points.
- Counter: avoid standing on arrival position; trail damage is moderate.

### Magistrate's Decree
- Channel lasting about 3 seconds, applies dual curses (Scorch + Wither).
- Wither reduces movement speed by 30%.
- Counter: move beyond 15 body lengths from boss; channel range is limited.

### Ashen Nova (Phase 2 trigger)
- Automatic burst at 55% HP threshold; deals fixed high damage regardless of position.
- Not avoidable; prepare with full HP and any available life flasks before 60% HP.
- Counter: ensure near-full HP buffer when approaching 60% threshold.

## Telegraphs And Counters
- Scroll animation before Ember Wave: step to side immediately.
- Raised staff before Scorch Curse: begin lateral movement.
- Vanish animation before Cinder Step: step away from predicted arrival position.
- Kneeling animation before Magistrate's Decree: increase distance to beyond 15 body lengths.
- Red screen border at 55% HP signals Ashen Nova: ensure full HP buffer.

## Positioning Rules
- Keep approximately 8-12 body lengths distance to stay outside Magistrate's Decree range.
- Move laterally and never stand still; static positioning accelerates curse stacking.
- Before 60% HP threshold, stop attacking and restore HP in preparation for Ashen Nova.

## Common Failure Modes
- Standing stationary during Scorch curse, receiving it, then eating amplified Ember Wave.
- Being in close range during Magistrate's Decree channel and taking both curses.
- Arriving at the 55% threshold at low HP and dying to the automatic Ashen Nova.

## Punish Windows
- After Ember Wave passes: 1.9 seconds.
- After Cinder Step arrival settles: 1.5 seconds.
- After Magistrate's Decree is broken by range: 2.1 seconds.
- After Ashen Nova resolves at transition: 3.0 second window.
