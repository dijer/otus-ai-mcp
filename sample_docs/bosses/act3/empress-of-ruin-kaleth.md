# Boss: Empress of Ruin Kaleth

## Act
- Act 3

## Role
- Act 3 final boss, multi-element pressure with arena collapse

## Encounter Summary
- Empress of Ruin Kaleth is the culmination of Act 3, combining elements introduced by all previous Act 3 bosses.
- She uses fire, ice, and chaos damage types in rapid alternating sequences.
- Three distinct phase transitions with full mechanic resets make this the most complex Act 3 encounter.

## Key Mechanics (Quick List)
- Crown Fire: radial fire nova centered on boss position.
- Frost Edict: ice lance barrage from above in cross pattern.
- Ruin Touch: melee grab with chaos damage burst on contact.
- Empire's Fall: column of light striking random player-adjacent zones.
- Final Edict (Phase 3): combines all elements in single cast sequence.

## Phase Flow
- Phase 1 (100% to 70%): fire and ice alternation; no chaos.
- Transition 1 (70%): Crown Fire automatic burst; prepare HP buffer at 75%.
- Phase 2 (70% to 40%): chaos added; Ruin Touch enters kit.
- Transition 2 (40%): Frost Edict automatic cross barrage.
- Phase 3 (40% to 0%): all elements active; Final Edict every 30 seconds.

## Core Mechanics

### Crown Fire
- Radial nova from boss position; inner ring deals more than outer ring.
- Counter: move to outer arena edge during radial; inner ring is lethal without fire resistance.

### Frost Edict
- Ice lance barrage from above in cross pattern (N/S/E/W columns).
- Gaps are diagonal positions (NE/SE/SW/NW).
- Counter: stand in diagonal position immediately on cast animation.

### Ruin Touch
- Melee grab attempt with 2-second reach radius.
- On contact: chaos damage burst; 1.5-second stagger.
- Counter: maintain 3-body-length minimum distance; back away on reach animation.

### Empire's Fall
- Three simultaneous columns strike at positions adjacent to player (not directly on player).
- Delay is 1.1 seconds after indicator appearance.
- Counter: move slightly toward boss to break adjacency pattern; columns follow player's last position.

### Final Edict (Phase 3)
- Every 30 seconds: Crown Fire, then Frost Edict, then Empire's Fall fire in rapid sequence.
- 2-second gap between each element.
- Counter: manage each element in sequence; do not move to next element's safe position before resolving current.

## Telegraphs And Counters
- Crown glow before Crown Fire: move to arena edge.
- Ice crystal formation above arena before Frost Edict: move to diagonal.
- Reaching arm animation before Ruin Touch: step back beyond 3 body lengths.
- Three ground glows adjacent to player before Empire's Fall: shift toward boss.

## Positioning Rules
- Track phase transitions; Crown Fire at 70% and Frost Edict at 40% are automatic.
- In Phase 3, anticipate Final Edict every 30 seconds and always have HP buffer ready.
- Stay mobile; static positioning is punished by all three damage types simultaneously.

## Common Failure Modes
- Taking auto Crown Fire at 70% at low HP.
- Standing in cross pattern during Frost Edict instead of diagonal.
- Getting grabbed by Ruin Touch during Empire's Fall evasion and taking both.
- Not managing 30-second Final Edict timer in Phase 3.

## Punish Windows
- After Crown Fire outer ring resolves: 2.0 seconds.
- After Frost Edict cross: 2.3 seconds.
- After failed Ruin Touch: 2.5 seconds.
- After Empire's Fall columns: 1.8 seconds.
- After Final Edict sequence completes: 4.0 seconds.

## Verification Facts
- Empress of Ruin Kaleth is the Act 3 final boss in this dataset.
- Crown Fire automatic burst fires at exactly 70% HP.
- Frost Edict automatic barrage fires at exactly 40% HP.
- Final Edict repeats every exactly 30 seconds in Phase 3.
