# Boss: Glacial Warden Soveth

## Act
- Act 3

## Role
- Ice and slow mechanic specialist, zone-control encounter

## Encounter Summary
- Glacial Warden Soveth is a mid-Act 3 encounter that tests movement control under increasing slow pressure.
- The arena progressively ices over, reducing safe footing.
- At 40% HP, Soveth enters Deep Freeze mode, locking the arena edge and pushing the fight into a narrow center zone.

## Key Mechanics (Quick List)
- Frost Lance: three-projectile linear volley with slight spread.
- Ice Sheet: places a persistent slippery ice patch at player's position.
- Glacial Shatter: stomp that shatters existing ice patches into shards.
- Blizzard Ring: ring of slow-moving ice orbs contracting inward.
- Deep Freeze (Phase 2): locks arena perimeter, pins fight to 50% of total floor space.

## Phase Flow
- Phase 1 (100% to about 40%): arena icing begins after first Ice Sheet; progressive floor hazard.
- Transition (around 40%): Deep Freeze activates, outer arena ring becomes impassable for 15 seconds intervals.
- Phase 2: faster Frost Lance, Ice Sheet on 4-second cooldown, Glacial Shatter becomes frequent.

## Core Mechanics

### Frost Lance
- Three lances fired in tight forward spread.
- Center lance aimed directly, outer two are 15 degrees off-center.
- Counter: step off-center; being directly in front results in all three hits.

### Ice Sheet
- Placed at player's current position after 0.5-second delay.
- Walking on ice sheet applies 40% movement slow for 2 seconds.
- Persists until Glacial Shatter destroys it.
- Counter: move immediately on placement; map ice locations to avoid accidental entry.

### Glacial Shatter
- Soveth stomps, shattering all ice patches into shards.
- Each shard travels outward from original ice patch position.
- Counter: move away from ice patches before stomp animation completes.

### Blizzard Ring
- Ring of 12 equidistant slow-moving ice orbs contracts from arena edge inward.
- Full contraction takes about 4 seconds.
- Single gap in ring at random position.
- Counter: identify gap location immediately, move to gap, wait for ring to pass.

### Deep Freeze (Phase 2)
- Outer arena ring freezes over and becomes impassable for 15 seconds, then thaws for 5 seconds.
- Pattern repeats throughout remainder of fight.
- Counter: memorize timing; use 5-second thaw windows to reset position.

## Telegraphs And Counters
- Arm extend before Frost Lance: step off-center.
- Ground shimmer before Ice Sheet: move away from current position.
- Raised foot animation before Glacial Shatter: move away from all ice patches.
- Sparkling perimeter animation before Blizzard Ring launch: locate gap immediately.
- Blue edge glow on arena wall signals Deep Freeze activation: move to center.

## Positioning Rules
- Memorize ice patch locations; never walk through them accidentally.
- Maintain central arena position before Deep Freeze activates.
- After Blizzard Ring, briefly check arena perimeter for open thaw windows.

## Common Failure Modes
- Walking into an old Ice Sheet during a dash and getting slowed during Frost Lance.
- Not identifying Blizzard Ring gap quickly and getting hit at full contraction.
- Being caught in outer zone during Deep Freeze activation.

## Punish Windows
- After Frost Lance: 2.1 seconds.
- After Ice Sheet placement: 1.8 seconds.
- After Glacial Shatter resolves: 2.4 seconds.
- After Blizzard Ring passes: 2.0 seconds.
