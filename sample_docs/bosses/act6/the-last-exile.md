# Boss: The Last Exile

## Act
- Act 6

## Role
- Act 6 final boss, full-kit mastery encounter

## Encounter Summary
- The Last Exile is the final campaign boss and tests mastery of all mechanics learned across Acts 1-6.
- Each phase introduces abilities modeled after all previous act bosses.
- Three full phases with no arena reset between them; HP and resources carry over.

## Key Mechanics (Quick List)
- Exile's Remembrance: cycle through abilities from acts 1-6 in structured order.
- Legacy Strike: damage scales with number of previous bosses whose mechanics appeared in the fight.
- Ruin Recall: summons a shadow copy of any previous act final boss for 12 seconds.
- Oblivion Surge: at 33% HP, removes all player buffs.
- True Ending: at 15% HP, all act boss shadows active simultaneously.

## Phase Flow
- Phase 1 (100% to 66%): Act 1-2 ability cycle.
- Phase 2 (66% to 33%): Act 3-4 ability cycle added; Ruin Recall begins.
- Phase 3 (33% to 0%): Oblivion Surge, all cycles active, True Ending at 15%.

## Punish Windows
- After Legacy Strike: 2.0 seconds.
- After Ruin Recall shadow expires: 3.0 seconds.
- After Oblivion Surge: 2.5 seconds.
- After True Ending shadows are cleared: 6.0 seconds final burst window.

## Verification Facts
- The Last Exile is the Act 6 final boss in this dataset.
- Oblivion Surge fires at exactly 33% HP and removes all player buffs.
- True Ending triggers at exactly 15% HP.
- Ruin Recall shadow copies last exactly 12 seconds.
