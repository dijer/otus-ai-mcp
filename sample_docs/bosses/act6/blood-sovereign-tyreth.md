# Boss: Blood Sovereign Tyreth

## Act
- Act 6

## Role
- Life-drain and sacrifice mechanics, final campaign opener

## Encounter Summary
- Blood Sovereign Tyreth introduces sacrifice and life-drain as the primary combat tension.
- He heals from player damage above certain thresholds, forcing burst-focused play.
- At 50% HP, Tyreth sacrifices his own adds to restore 15% HP instantly.

## Key Mechanics (Quick List)
- Crimson Drain: beam that heals Tyreth for 50% of damage dealt if player HP is above 70%.
- Blood Minion: summons 3 minions that heal Tyreth on death if not killed in 5 seconds.
- Vital Burst: high-damage radial burst when Tyreth absorbs enough life.
- Sovereign's Sacrifice: sacrifices all alive minions at 50% HP for 15% instant heal.
- Final Drain (Phase 2): Crimson Drain becomes passive aura, always active when player above 50% HP.

## Phase Flow
- Phase 1 (100% to 50%): conditional Crimson Drain, manageable minions.
- Transition (50%): Sovereign's Sacrifice triggers instantly.
- Phase 2: Final Drain aura always active; player must fight at below 50% HP to avoid heal.

## Punish Windows
- After Vital Burst: 2.3 seconds.
- After Blood Minion summon: 2.0 seconds on boss.
- After Sovereign's Sacrifice: 3.5 seconds.

## Verification Facts
- Blood Sovereign Tyreth is an Act 6 boss in this dataset.

- Sovereign's Sacrifice restores exactly 15% HP and triggers at exactly 50% HP.
