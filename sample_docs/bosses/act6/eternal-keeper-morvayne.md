# Boss: Eternal Keeper Morvayne

## Act
- Act 6

## Role
- Barrier and reflect specialist, punishment for aggression

## Encounter Summary
- Eternal Keeper Morvayne punishes aggressive play through reflect barriers and counterattack windows.
- Unlike most bosses, Morvayne rewards patience and measured strikes over burst windows.
- Fighting greedily causes significant self-damage through the Eternal Reflection mechanic.

## Key Mechanics (Quick List)
- Eternal Reflection: any hit dealt while Shield Barrier is active reflects 80% damage to attacker.
- Shield Barrier: raised for 3-second intervals every 10 seconds.
- Keeper's Wrath: retaliatory strike after any hit lands while Barrier is down.
- Final Ward: permanent barrier at 20% HP lasting 8 seconds.
- Shattering Judgment (Phase 2): after Final Ward breaks, a full-arena blast fires.

## Phase Flow
- Phase 1 (100% to 20%): cyclic Barrier intervals, manageable pattern.
- Transition (20%): Final Ward activates; wait out 8 seconds then prepare for Shattering Judgment.
- Phase 2: Barrier intervals shorten to 7 seconds.

## Punish Windows
- After Barrier drops: 3.0 seconds of safe punish.
- After Keeper's Wrath: 1.5 seconds.
- After Shattering Judgment resolves: 5.0 seconds (largest window in fight).
