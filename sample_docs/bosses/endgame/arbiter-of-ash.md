# Boss: Arbiter of Ash

## Tier
- Endgame

## Role
- Pinnacle-style endgame pressure boss.

## Signature Mechanics
- Ash Verdict: delayed ground detonation after a line telegraph.
- Cinder Orbit: rotating ember projectiles that collapse inward.
- Final Judgement: execute-style cast if the player remains in overlap zones.
- Spear Dash Variant: rapid linear engage into close follow-up.
- Aerial Slam Variant: short air rise into impact AoE.

## Encounter Summary
- Multi-phase pinnacle fight with fast transitions and heavy overlap pressure.
- Mechanical focus: reading telegraphs, preserving movement tools, and controlling arena geometry.

## Key Mechanics (Quick List)
- Ash Verdict delayed detonation line pattern.
- Cinder Orbit rotating projectiles with inward collapse.
- Final Judgement execute pressure in overlap zones.
- Spear Dash and thrust chains with short punish windows.
- Phase 2 rain-circle pattern requiring gap positioning.

## Phase Flow
- Phase 1 (100% to 50%): baseline dash, thrust, slam, and orbit pressure.
- Mid pressure (50% to about 40%): keeps baseline kit and adds more ambient hazard orbs.
- Phase 2 trigger (around 40%): recovery spike, faster chaining, denser overlap patterns.

## Attack Groups And Counters
- Dash and Thrust group: side-dodge late, then punish briefly from flank.
- Upper slash into lunge group: break line-of-attack and do not roll backward.
- Aerial Slam group: pre-move from marked zone before impact pulse.
- Ranged burst group: dodge first two projectiles, then reposition for beam lane.
- Rain pattern group (phase 2): stand in gaps between circle markers, then rotate early.

## Timings
- Safe punish after Ash Verdict: about 1.1 seconds.
- Reposition window during Cinder Orbit: about 1.6 seconds.
- Typical punish after missed Dash Thrust: about 0.7 to 0.9 seconds.

## Positioning Rules
- Prefer diagonal movement to break linear dash vectors.
- Avoid hard-committing to arena edges during orbit collapse.
- Keep one instant mobility tool reserved for Final Judgement or Rain overlap.

## Common Failure Modes
- Rolling backward into beam lane after ranged opener.
- Greeding damage inside orbit collapse timing.
- Spending all mobility before phase 2 rain sequence.

## Practice Notes
- Move diagonally through orbit gaps, do not backpedal in straight lines.
