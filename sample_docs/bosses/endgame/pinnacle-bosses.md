# Path of Exile 2: Endgame Boss Encounters — Additional Pinnacle Bosses

## Overview

Beyond Arbiter of Ash, the endgame contains multiple Pinnacle boss encounters accessible through specific invitation systems. Each encounter has unique access requirements, mechanics, and reward pools.

---

## Searing Exarch

### Access
- Collect 10 Formed Invitation pieces from maps in the Maven's Atlas.
- Combine in the Map Device with a Formed Invitation.

### Role
- Fire and Expedition-themed boss.
- Tests ability to handle persistent fire ground zones.

### Key Mechanics (Quick List)
- Searing Beam: long linear beam rotating clockwise.
- Crucible Slam: overhead slam with delayed fire pool.
- Exarch's Blessing: random arena square burns at high intensity for 4 seconds.
- Scorched Earth: after 40% HP, floor tiles begin permanently activating in sequence.
- Final Judgment: at 20% HP, all remaining floor tiles ignite simultaneously for 3 seconds.

### Phase Flow
- Phase 1 (100% to 40%): standard rotation with manageable floor burn.
- Transition (40%): Scorched Earth begins sequential tile activation.
- Phase 2: tile sequence adds to standard rotation; floor management becomes critical.
- Final Judgment (20%): survive 3-second all-tile ignition, then finish.

### Positioning Rules
- Map which tiles have been burned; burned tiles do not re-activate.
- Follow Searing Beam rotation clockwise, maintaining distance.
- Pre-position to unburned tiles before Scorched Earth sequence.

### Punish Windows
- After Searing Beam rotates past: 1.5 seconds.
- After Crucible Slam pool fades: 2.0 seconds.
- After Exarch's Blessing tile expires: 1.8 seconds.
- After Final Judgment: 5.0 seconds (final burst window).

### Rewards
- Unique items: The Searing Exarch Unique Pool (fire-themed).
- Scarabs: Exarch Scarab type.
- Stacked Decks: above-average rate.

### Verification Facts
- Searing Exarch requires exactly 10 Formed Invitation pieces.
- Final Judgment triggers at exactly 20% HP.
- Scorched Earth sequential activation begins at exactly 40% HP.

## Eater of Worlds

### Access
- Collect 10 Twisted Invitation pieces from maps.
- Combine in Map Device with Twisted Invitation.

### Role
- Chaos and cold hybrid; tentacle and consumption mechanics.

### Key Mechanics (Quick List)
- Tentacle Slam: ground tentacle spawns at player position with 1.2-second delay.
- Devouring Maw: pulls player toward boss at high velocity; requires movement skill to resist.
- Void Rain: chaos projectiles falling from above covering 60% of arena.
- Elder Presence: spawns shadow copies of previous Shaper-adjacent bosses.
- World's End: at 15% HP, three simultaneous Tentacle Slams fire at player.

### Phase Flow
- Phase 1 (100% to 50%): Tentacle Slam and Void Rain rotation.
- Phase 2 (50% to 15%): Elder Presence adds shadow copeis.
- Phase 3 (15%): World's End activated; DPS race to kill.

### Positioning Rules
- Reserve movement skill exclusively for Devouring Maw; do not waste it on Tentacle Slam.
- Void Rain has clear gaps; map them after first cast.
- Kill shadow copies from Elder Presence before re-engaging main boss.

### Punish Windows
- After Tentacle Slam misses: 2.0 seconds.
- After Devouring Maw releases: 3.0 seconds.
- After Void Rain ends: 2.5 seconds.

### Verification Facts
- Eater of Worlds requires exactly 10 Twisted Invitation pieces.
- World's End triggers at exactly 15% HP.
- Devouring Maw pull velocity requires a movement skill to resist.

## The Shaper

### Access
- Complete all 15 Shaper Guardian Maps.
- Combine 4 Shaper Fragments in Map Device.

### Role
- Classic pinnacle encounter; space and void themed.
- Multiple teleport phases and clone mechanics.

### Key Mechanics (Quick List)
- Shaper Beam: slow-rotating death beam.
- Bullet Hell: large projectile burst from center.
- Ball Explosion: fires 6 exploding orbs outward from position.
- Clone Phase: teleports to center, spawns 4 clones, disappears until all clones die.
- Shaper Slam: overhead air slam with large ground AoE; arrives at 33% HP.
- Final Form: at 10% HP, all previously seen mechanics fire simultaneously once.

### Phase Flow
- Phase 1 (100% to 70%): beam rotation and projectile management.
- Phase 2 (70% to 33%): Clone Phase added; ball explosions become frequent.
- Phase 3 (33% to 10%): Shaper Slam added; arena pressure dramatically increases.
- Final Form (10%): Single combined all-mechanic sequence before death.

### Positioning Rules
- Shaper Beam rotates predictably; move counter-beam direction.
- Clone Phase: kill clones in a consistent order; randomized clone kill leads to missed patterns.
- Shaper Slam: move to outer arena edge on slam wind-up.
- Final Form: prioritize dodge over damage; 10% can be burned before combination sequence.

### Punish Windows
- After Shaper Beam rotation completes: 2.5 seconds.
- After Bullet Hell burst: 2.0 seconds.
- After all clones die: 4.5 seconds burst window on main Shaper.
- After Shaper Slam: 3.0 seconds.
- After Final Form all-mechanic sequence: 6.0 seconds.

### Rewards
- Shaper Unique Pool: BiS items for multiple build archetypes.
- Shaped Items: Base items with Shaper influence mods available.

### Verification Facts
- The Shaper requires exactly 4 Shaper Fragments to access.
- Clone Phase spawns exactly 4 simultaneous clones.
- Final Form triggers at exactly 10% HP.
- Shaper Beam rotation direction is counter-clockwise.

## The Elder

### Access
- Dominate the Atlas by running Shaper-adjacent maps to expand Elder influence.
- Elder appears on the Atlas when 4 quadrants are fully influenced.

### Role
- Physical and cold damage; tentacle and drain mechanics.
- Contrast to The Shaper: slower and more methodical.

### Key Mechanics (Quick List)
- Cold Snap: places chilled zone at player's position.
- Tentacle Web: places web that slows player inside it.
- Shockwave Slam: rippling physical shockwave from Elder's position.
- Drain: beam that drains player life to restore Elder HP.
- Guardian Summon: summons one of four Elder Guardians as an assist.
- Death Embrace: at 5% HP, Elder Guardians all resurrect simultaneously.

### Phase Flow
- Phase 1 (100% to 50%): standard rotation with occasional Guardian.
- Phase 2 (50% to 5%): increased Guardian frequency; Drain becomes more common.
- Death Embrace (5%): all 4 Guardians resurrect; killing all 4 immediately finishes Elder.

### Punish Windows
- After Cold Snap: 1.8 seconds.
- After Shockwave Slam: 2.5 seconds.
- After Drain beam is broken by range: 2.0 seconds.
- After each Guardian kill during Death Embrace: 1.0 second.

### Verification Facts
- The Elder Death Embrace triggers at exactly 5% HP.
- Death Embrace resurrects exactly all 4 Elder Guardians simultaneously.
- Elder Drain beam can be broken by moving beyond 14 body lengths.

## Maven (Pinnacle Form)

### Access
- Witness The Maven watching all 10 Atlas bosses in a single session.
- Maven sends an invitation after completing the witnessing.

### Role
- Memory and pattern testing; the most mechanically demanding encounter.

### Key Mechanics (Quick List)
- Maven's Gaze: targeted beam that tracks player, deals mounting damage per tick.
- Memory Bomb: delayed explosion at player's last recorded position.
- Witnessed Ability: uses a copied ability from any boss previously witnessed.
- Brain Pattern: drops 9 glowing spheres; player must touch them without missing or re-touching.
- Final Witness: at 15% HP, copies 3 abilities simultaneously.

### Phase Flow
- Phase 1 (100% to 50%): standard rotation with Memory Bombs.
- Phase 2 (50% to 15%): Witnessed Abilities active; Brain Pattern frequency doubles.
- Final Witness (15%): simultaneous 3-ability activation.

### Positioning Rules
- Maven's Gaze follows slowly; lateral movement is sufficient.
- Memory Bomb always drops at position 0.5 seconds after cast; keep moving.
- Brain Pattern: touch spheres at outer arc first, then work inward.
- Final Witness: use all available mobility tools.

### Punish Windows
- After Maven's Gaze ends: 2.0 seconds.
- After Memory Bomb detonation: 1.5 seconds.
- After Brain Pattern completes: 4.0 seconds.
- After Final Witness sequence: 6.0 seconds.

### Verification Facts
- Maven requires witnessing exactly 10 Atlas bosses in one session.
- Brain Pattern drops exactly 9 spheres.
- Final Witness triggers at exactly 15% HP and copies exactly 3 abilities simultaneously.

## General Pinnacle Preparation Checklist

### Resistances
- All elements capped at 75%+.
- Chaos resistance at 0%+.
- Armor or evasion mitigation layer active.

### Life or Energy Shield
- Life: minimum 5,000 for entry pinnacle; 6,500+ for Shaper/Maven.
- Energy Shield (if CI): minimum 8,000.

### Damage Output
- Able to kill entry pinnacle bosses within 8-12 minutes.
- Shaper and Maven: target 6-8 minute kill for Phase 3 management.

### Movement Skills
- At least one instant movement skill.
- At least 2 mobility uses per encounter phase.

### Flask Setup
- Life/ES recovery flask with surgeon's prefix.
- One utility flask appropriate for the boss element.
- Quicksilver or movement-enhancing flask.
