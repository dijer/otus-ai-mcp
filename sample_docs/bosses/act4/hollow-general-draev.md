# Boss: Hollow General Draev

## Act
- Act 4

## Role
- Undead commander, army-and-boss simultaneous pressure

## Encounter Summary
- Hollow General Draev commands undead waves throughout the fight, creating a dual threat of adds and direct boss pressure.
- Killing adds too slowly results in overwhelming stacks; killing them too fast triggers a rage buff on Draev.
- Optimal play involves managing a mid-range add count of 3-5 active adds.

## Key Mechanics (Quick List)
- Deathblow Strike: leaping overhead smash with shockwave.
- Undying Legion: passively summons 1 undead add every 6 seconds.
- Legion Rage: buff triggered when fewer than 2 adds are alive, increasing all damage by 40%.
- Gravewarden Charge: charges through entire arena, passing through adds without stopping.
- Final Order (Phase 2): summons 6 adds simultaneously at 35% HP.

## Phase Flow
- Phase 1 (100% to 35%): steady add accumulation, standard boss pressure.
- Transition (35%): Final Order summons 6 adds simultaneously; burst window immediately after.
- Phase 2: adds summoned every 4 seconds instead of 6; Legion Rage threshold raises to 4 adds.

## Core Mechanics

### Deathblow Strike
- Draev leaps to player position and slams down.
- Shockwave ring expands outward from impact.
- Counter: dodge away from landing position, step back from ring expansion.

### Undying Legion
- One add every 6 seconds from arena edges.
- Adds deal light damage but apply Decay stacks on contact (slows movement at 5 stacks).
- Counter: maintain 3-4 active adds; kill one whenever stack count threatens.

### Legion Rage
- Triggers if fewer than 2 adds are alive.
- 40% damage increase on all Draev attacks; lasts until 3 adds are alive again.
- Counter: never kill adds below the safe floor count; accept moderate add pressure.

### Gravewarden Charge
- Full-arena linear charge at player's position.
- Passes through all adds.
- Counter: dodge perpendicular; adds in charge path take no damage.

### Final Order (Phase 2 trigger)
- 6 adds spawn simultaneously at 35% HP.
- Counter: burn down 4 of the 6 quickly to drop below Legion Rage threshold but stay above it.

## Punish Windows
- After Deathblow shockwave: 2.0 seconds.
- After Gravewarden Charge: 1.8 seconds during deceleration.
- After Final Order spawn completes: 2.5 seconds burst window on boss.
