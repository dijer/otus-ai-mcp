# Boss: Worldbreaker Gorthund

## Act
- Act 4

## Role
- Act 4 final boss, seismic and crushing force encounter

## Encounter Summary
- Worldbreaker Gorthund is a titanic construct boss that reshapes the arena throughout the fight.
- He destroys arena sections with each major ability, progressively narrowing available ground.
- Unlike Vorath the Unmade, Gorthund's arena destruction is targeted; he aims abilities at the player.

## Key Mechanics (Quick List)
- Cataclysm Strike: floor-shattering overhead slam that destroys a 3x3 tile zone.
- Tremor Wave: ground roll moving outward, low height.
- Pillar Collapse: knocks down arena pillars that become obstacles.
- Seismic Roar: massive AoE shockwave at 45% HP.
- World's End (Phase 2): doubles ability frequency; Cataclysm Strike destroys 5x5 zones.

## Phase Flow
- Phase 1 (100% to 45%): arena slowly losing tiles from Cataclysm Strikes.
- Transition (45%): Seismic Roar auto-fires; must be at full HP.
- Phase 2: World's End applies; arena destruction accelerates.

## Punish Windows
- After Cataclysm Strike misses: 2.2 seconds.
- After Tremor Wave passes: 1.9 seconds.
- After Pillar Collapse: 3.0 seconds (longest window in fight).
- After Seismic Roar resolves: 4.5 second window.

## Verification Facts
- Worldbreaker Gorthund is the Act 4 final boss in this dataset.
- Seismic Roar fires automatically at exactly 45% HP.
- Phase 2 Cataclysm Strike destroys 5x5 tile zones.
- Phase 1 Cataclysm Strike destroys 3x3 tile zones.
