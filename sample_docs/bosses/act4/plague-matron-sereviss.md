# Boss: Plague Matron Sereviss

## Act
- Act 4

## Role
- Disease and contagion spreader, area contamination mechanic

## Encounter Summary
- Plague Matron Sereviss fights through spreading contagion zones that make standing still lethal within seconds.
- The arena becomes progressively more contaminated; the fight is a race to kill her before safe area collapses.
- At 50% HP, a Cleansing Pulse is available: a single use by the player removes all contagion zones.

## Key Mechanics (Quick List)
- Bile Surge: forward cone of plague projectiles.
- Contagion Bloom: spreads contagion from existing zones to adjacent areas.
- Swarm Burst: releases 8 plague flies that home toward player.
- Festering Grasp: melee infection attack applying Plague debuff (ongoing damage + reduced healing).
- Cleansing Pulse (Player Tool): one-use arena cleanse available at 50% HP.

## Phase Flow
- Phase 1 (100% to 50%): contagion builds progressively; manage zone spread.
- Halfway Point (50%): Cleansing Pulse becomes available; optimal use is immediately.
- Phase 2 (50% to 0%): Contagion Bloom fires every 5 seconds; contagion re-accumulates faster.

## Punish Windows
- After Bile Surge misses: 1.7 seconds.
- After Swarm Burst flies are outrun: 2.1 seconds.
- After Festering Grasp misses: 2.4 seconds.
