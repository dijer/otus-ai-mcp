# Boss: Ironclad Ravager

## Act
- Act 2

## Role
- Heavy armor juggernaut, physical pressure encounter

## Encounter Summary
- Ironclad Ravager is a slow but relentless armored bruiser who creates unavoidable pressure through persistent terrain control.
- The encounter punishes poor positioning and rewards patient burst timing during stagger windows.
- Fight transitions at around 35% HP into a berserk mode with reduced armor and increased speed.

## Key Mechanics (Quick List)
- Ground Slam: massive AoE stomp with shockwave ring expanding outward.
- Shoulder Charge: linear charge across full arena width.
- Iron Wall: temporary shield that reflects projectiles back.
- Rubble Throw: arc-trajectory boulder projectile.
- Berserk Surge (Phase 2): armor shatters, movement speed increases sharply, all abilities fire 35% faster.

## Phase Flow
- Phase 1 (100% to about 35%): methodical pace, heavy single hits, predictable patterning.
- Transition (around 35%): armor shatter animation, brief stagger window of approximately 2.1 seconds.
- Phase 2: faster pacing, Berserk Surge applies to all abilities, Ground Slam radius increases by 25%.

## Core Mechanics

### Ground Slam
- Single stomp creating a shockwave ring that expands outward over 1.5 seconds.
- Ring deals heavy physical damage on contact.
- Center of slam is always safe after initial impact.
- Counter: run into the center (toward boss) during ring expansion, then punish immediately.

### Shoulder Charge
- Linear full-arena charge telegraphed by turning to face player and lowering shoulder.
- Deals knockback plus brief stun on contact.
- Leaves a short after-window where boss decelerates.
- Counter: dodge perpendicular to charge direction, punish during deceleration.

### Iron Wall
- Boss raises shield facing player direction, reflecting all ranged attacks for 3 seconds.
- No movement during Iron Wall activation.
- Counter: switch to melee or move to boss flank; Iron Wall only covers frontal 120-degree arc.

### Rubble Throw
- Picks up rubble and throws in high arc toward player's position at time of throw.
- Impact creates a small debris field lasting 2 seconds.
- Counter: move from position as soon as throw animation begins; debris field is small.

### Berserk Surge (Phase 2 only)
- Triggered at 35% HP by armor shattering.
- Removes armor reflection mechanic from Iron Wall.
- All animations accelerated; Ground Slam shockwave expands 25% faster.
- Counter: adapt burst timing to shorter punish windows; keep two mobility skills ready.

## Telegraphs And Counters
- Wide arm spread before Ground Slam: begin moving toward boss immediately.
- Turning body 90 degrees before Shoulder Charge: prep perpendicular dodge.
- Slow arm raise before Iron Wall: switch attack mode for 3 seconds.
- High overhead windup before Rubble Throw: abandon current position.

## Positioning Rules
- Mid-range positioning is optimal; melee range risks Ground Slam, max range loses Shoulder Charge dodge timing.
- After each Ground Slam, a safe burst window opens; use it consistently.
- During Phase 2, reduce gap-closing time; punish windows shrink significantly.

## Common Failure Modes
- Rolling away from Ground Slam and hitting the ring at peak expansion.
- Continuing ranged attacks during Iron Wall and receiving full reflect damage.
- Underestimating burst window duration in Phase 2 and overcommitting.

## Punish Windows
- After Ground Slam center: 2.0 seconds of free punish.
- After Shoulder Charge deceleration: 1.7 seconds.
- After Rubble Throw impact: 1.4 seconds.
- Phase 2 stagger window at transition: 2.1 seconds.
