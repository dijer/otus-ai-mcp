# Boss: Swamp Sovereign Urrath

## Act
- Act 2

## Role
- Environmental hazard controller, area denial specialist

## Encounter Summary
- Swamp Sovereign Urrath combines slow hard-hitting strikes with progressive arena flooding.
- The fight becomes increasingly difficult as the swamp level rises, limiting safe movement zones.
- Players must balance damage output with awareness of rising water levels at 70%, 50%, and 30% HP thresholds.

## Key Mechanics (Quick List)
- Tide Slam: massive ground pound triggering flood surge in targeted zone.
- Mire Grasp: ground tendrils rising from swamp areas to root the player.
- Undertow Pull: pulls player toward swamp center with gradually increasing force.
- Bellow Call: calls three swamp-spawn adds during phase transitions.
- Surge Overflow (Phase 2): all swamp zones connect, halving safe ground area.

## Phase Flow
- Phase 1 (100% to 70%): one active swamp zone in arena corner, standard toolkit.
- First Threshold (70%): second swamp zone appears opposite corner, Bellow Call triggers.
- Second Threshold (50%): third swamp zone expands from side wall, Undertow Pull gains reach.
- Third Threshold (30%): Surge Overflow triggers, connecting all zones; boss speed increases.

## Core Mechanics

### Tide Slam
- Marks target zone with slow ripple indicator before impact.
- Delay between mark and impact is about 1.8 seconds.
- Hitting a swamp zone with Tide Slam permanently deepens that zone, increasing Mire Grasp frequency.
- Counter: exit marked zone immediately on ripple appearance; do not re-enter for 2 seconds after impact.

### Mire Grasp
- Tendrils emerge from active swamp ground within 3 body lengths of player.
- Root duration is 1.4 seconds; can be broken early with movement skills.
- Frequency increases each time Tide Slam deepens the swamp.
- Counter: stay out of swamp edges; use movement skill to break root immediately.

### Undertow Pull
- Periodic radial pull toward swamp center every 8 seconds.
- Pull force scales with number of active swamp zones.
- Counter: pre-position away from swamp center before pull interval; use movement against pull direction.

### Bellow Call
- Summons three Swamp Spawn adds at 70% HP.
- Adds are low HP but apply slow debuff on contact.
- Counter: burst one add quickly, kite other two; adds despawn after 20 seconds if not killed.

### Surge Overflow (Phase 2 at 30%)
- All three swamp zones merge, covering approximately 60% of total arena floor.
- Remaining safe ground is scattered in patches.
- Mire Grasp frequency doubles in merged zone.
- Counter: memorize safe patch locations before 30% threshold; plan movement between patches.

## Telegraphs And Counters
- Ripple ring on ground before Tide Slam: exit zone within 1.5 seconds.
- Subtle bubbling effect on swamp edge before Mire Grasp: avoid swamp perimeter.
- Low rumble sound cue every 8 seconds before Undertow Pull: position against pull direction.
- Boss rears back and opens mouth before Bellow Call: prepare to switch to add management.

## Positioning Rules
- Always maintain awareness of which arena zones are safe ground and which are swamp.
- Map the three safe patches mentally before the fight enters third threshold.
- Avoid the swamp center at all times; it is the focal point of both Undertow Pull and Mire Grasp.
- Stand near edge of safe ground, not in center of it, to preserve movement options.

## Common Failure Modes
- Getting rooted by Mire Grasp while standing in swamp edge, then taking Tide Slam.
- Killing adds slowly and letting their slow stacks accumulate during critical burst phases.
- Not repositioning to memorized safe patches before Surge Overflow at 30%.

## Punish Windows
- After Tide Slam misses: 2.5 seconds of free punish near boss.
- After Bellow Call add spawn completes: 2.0 seconds before boss resumes attacks.
- After Undertow Pull resolves: 1.3 seconds of light punish opportunity.
