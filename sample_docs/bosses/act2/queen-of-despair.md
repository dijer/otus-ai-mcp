# Boss: Queen of Despair

## Act
- Act 2

## Role
- Mid-act poison and area-denial gatekeeper

## Encounter Summary
- Queen of Despair is a wide-area poison caster who punishes static play aggressively.
- The fight rewards continuous lateral movement and careful timing of burst windows.
- She transitions at around 50% HP into an accelerated phase with overlapping poison clouds.

## Key Mechanics (Quick List)
- Venom Burst: radial poison spray from her position, six directions.
- Seeping Ground: places persistent toxic pool under player's last known position.
- Corrosive Shriek: frontal cone attack with short delay before impact.
- Spore Rain: slow falling spore clusters that linger for around 4 seconds.
- Frenzied Surge (Phase 2): rapid repositioning charge followed by stacking Venom Burst.

## Phase Flow
- Phase 1 (100% to about 50% HP): methodical poison zones, frequent Seeping Ground usage.
- Transition (around 50% HP): short frenzy window, arena fills with three simultaneous Seeping Grounds.
- Phase 2: all Phase 1 tools at higher frequency plus Frenzied Surge added to kit.

## Core Mechanics

### Venom Burst
- Six-direction radial spray fired simultaneously.
- Each spray leaves a small persistent puddle at endpoint.
- Counter: position between two rays, then rotate after burst resolves.

### Seeping Ground
- Dropped at player's position after a short 0.6-second delay.
- Stays active for about 5 seconds.
- Counter: keep moving; never stop in one spot for more than 1 second during active fight.

### Corrosive Shriek
- 90-degree frontal cone with a clear wind-up animation.
- Damage applies a corrosion debuff that amplifies all subsequent poison intake by 20%.
- Counter: step sideways on wind-up, punish after cone resolves.

### Spore Rain
- Clusters appear above random arena positions and take around 1.2 seconds to land.
- Shadow indicators on ground before impact.
- Counter: read shadow, step away before landing; do not sprint away as distance is short.

### Frenzied Surge (Phase 2 only)
- Three rapid-repositioning charges toward player position.
- Each charge ends with a small Venom Burst.
- Counter: dodge through each charge late, not early; keep distance between bursts.

## Telegraphs And Counters
- She raises both arms overhead before Venom Burst: sidestep during raise animation.
- She lowers head and inhales before Corrosive Shriek: move perpendicular immediately.
- Shadow clusters on ground with distinct purple tint precede Spore Rain: move to unoccupied zone.
- After phase transition frenzy, she briefly pauses: use that 1.5-second window for burst damage.

## Positioning Rules
- Never stand in center of arena during Phase 1; Seeping Ground makes center lethal quickly.
- Use arena rim but keep 2-3 body lengths from walls to preserve lateral escape options.
- In Phase 2, rotate clockwise to stay ahead of accumulated pools.

## Common Failure Modes
- Standing still during Seeping Ground placement and getting caught by the delayed drop.
- Using a long-channel attack into the phase transition burst, getting interrupted mid-channel.
- Running directly away from Corrosive Shriek instead of sidestepping.

## Punish Windows
- After full Venom Burst resolves and puddles settle: 1.8 seconds of safe melee.
- After Corrosive Shriek misses: 2.2 seconds of punish opportunity.
- After Spore Rain sequence ends: 1.5 seconds before next ability.
- After each Frenzied Surge charge (Phase 2): 0.7 seconds between charges.

## Positioning Notes for Phase 2
- The accumulated Seeping Grounds from Phase 2 entry can cover up to 40% of the arena floor by the final third of the fight.
- Prioritize arena management over damage; a clean kill at reduced DPS is better than a wipe.

## Verification Facts
- Queen of Despair appears in Act 2 of this dataset.
- Her phase transition occurs at approximately 50% HP.
- Frenzied Surge is exclusive to Phase 2.
- The Corrosive Shriek debuff amplifies poison damage intake by exactly 20%.
