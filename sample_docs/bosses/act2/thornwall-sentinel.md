# Boss: Thornwall Sentinel

## Act
- Act 2

## Role
- Ranged siege defender, projectile pattern specialist

## Encounter Summary
- Thornwall Sentinel is a stationary-adjacent boss who occupies defensive positions and attacks through layered projectile patterns.
- The fight rewards reading multi-hit salvo timing and punishing after each salvo sequence.
- Sentinel repositions to a new defensive post at 60% and 25% HP.

## Key Mechanics (Quick List)
- Thorn Volley: wide-spread projectile fan with 7 simultaneous projectiles.
- Siege Bolt: single precise high-damage bolt targeting last known player position.
- Root Web: places a circular thorn trap that triggers on player entry.
- Barrier Stance: temporary invulnerability while repositioning, lasting 2.5 seconds.
- Final Salvo (Phase 2): at 25% HP fires triple Thorn Volley in rapid succession.

## Phase Flow
- Phase 1 (100% to 60%): standard projectile patterns from initial position.
- First Reposition (60%): Barrier Stance activates, Sentinel moves to second post.
- Phase 2 (60% to 25%): increased Siege Bolt frequency, Root Web placement doubles.
- Second Reposition (25%): Barrier Stance activates, Final Salvo begins as signature Phase 3 opener.

## Core Mechanics

### Thorn Volley
- Seven-projectile fan covering approximately 140-degree arc.
- All projectiles travel at equal speed.
- Gaps between projectiles are consistent and wide enough to step through.
- Counter: position in gap between outer projectile and second-outermost; step into gap on fire.

### Siege Bolt
- Single high-speed bolt aimed at player's exact position at cast time.
- Deals triple damage compared to Thorn Volley single projectile.
- No AoE on impact; purely single-target.
- Counter: sidestep immediately on cast animation; the bolt has no tracking.

### Root Web
- Placed at random arena positions, not directly targeted at player.
- Invisible until player enters radius of about 1.5 body lengths.
- Triggers a 1.8-second root plus ongoing thorn damage.
- Counter: move carefully through unfamiliar arena sections; learn trap positions.

### Barrier Stance
- Activates at 60% and 25% HP when Sentinel repositions.
- Lasts exactly 2.5 seconds; all damage is nullified.
- Boss moves to next position during Barrier Stance.
- Counter: reposition yourself, not the boss; use the 2.5 seconds to set up your next burst position.

### Final Salvo (Phase 2, 25% trigger)
- Three consecutive Thorn Volleys fired at 0.8-second intervals.
- Combined coverage makes full dodge difficult without movement skills.
- Counter: use movement skill on second volley; first and third can be dodged manually.

## Telegraphs And Counters
- Wings spread before Thorn Volley: identify your gap before projectiles leave.
- Single arm raised before Siege Bolt: sidestep in any direction.
- Ground shimmer at random location before Root Web placement: avoid area.
- Glow animation on boss before Barrier Stance: accept invulnerability, reposition yourself.

## Positioning Rules
- Mid-range is optimal to have time to read both Thorn Volley gaps and Siege Bolt.
- After each Barrier Stance reposition, immediately survey new trap Web locations.
- Do not rush the boss during Barrier Stance; it wastes time.

## Common Failure Modes
- Moving directly into a gap and triggering a Root Web inside the gap.
- Using mobility skill on first Final Salvo volley and having nothing left for the second.
- Continuing attacks during Barrier Stance instead of repositioning.

## Punish Windows
- After full Thorn Volley resolves: 2.0 seconds of punish.
- After Siege Bolt impact: 1.6 seconds.
- After Root Web fails to trigger: 1.2 seconds.
