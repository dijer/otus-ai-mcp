# Boss: Vexara the Coiled

## Act
- Act 2

## Role
- Speed-based serpentine duelist, act 2 final boss

## Encounter Summary
- Vexara the Coiled is the final challenge of Act 2, combining extreme mobility with venom burst windows.
- The fight is primarily a reaction test: each ability must be read and countered in under 0.8 seconds.
- Vexara transitions at approximately 45% HP into an enraged form with doubled movement speed.

## Key Mechanics (Quick List)
- Serpent Dash: rapid coil-launch charge in straight line.
- Venom Spit Spread: triple-stream venom projectile, partially tracking.
- Death Coil: slow wrap-around grab attempt, easily avoided.
- Tail Sweep: backward 180-degree arc sweep after dash.
- Enrage (Phase 2): movement speed doubles, Tail Sweep becomes 360-degree spin.

## Phase Flow
- Phase 1 (100% to about 45%): standard pattern with clear telegraphs, generous punish windows.
- Enrage Transition (around 45%): brief coil animation lasting 1.2 seconds.
- Phase 2: all Phase 1 patterns at higher frequency, Tail Sweep upgrade, shorter punish windows.

## Core Mechanics

### Serpent Dash
- Launches from coiled position directly at player.
- Travel time is extremely fast; approximately 0.3 seconds for full arena width.
- Counter: dodge perpendicular at the moment of launch, not at impact; reaction window is tight.

### Venom Spit Spread
- Three venom streams: one aimed at player, one 30 degrees left, one 30 degrees right.
- Center stream has slight tracking for about 0.5 seconds before locking in.
- Counter: move to extreme left or right to avoid all three streams cleanly.

### Death Coil
- Slow wrap attempt telegraphed by wide circular approach.
- Grab animation is about 1.5 seconds total, clearly visible.
- Counter: walk away in any direction; this ability is present mainly to bait panic mobility usage.

### Tail Sweep
- After each Serpent Dash, the tail sweeps backward covering 180 degrees behind boss.
- Punish window before Tail Sweep resolves is only 0.6 seconds.
- Counter: punish briefly after dash then disengage before Tail Sweep completes.

### Enrage Phase 2
- Doubled movement speed changes dash distance and timing significantly.
- Tail Sweep upgrades to 360-degree spin after dash.
- Counter: punish window after Serpent Dash in Phase 2 shrinks to 0.4 seconds; only one hit, then retreat.

## Telegraphs And Counters
- Tight coil posture before Serpent Dash: begin dodge immediately, before launch.
- Head bobbing before Venom Spit Spread: start lateral movement.
- Circular approach before Death Coil: walk away calmly.
- Glowing tail before Tail Sweep: disengage after single punish hit.

## Positioning Rules
- Stay at mid-range to maximize Serpent Dash dodge window.
- Do not chase Vexara; let her come to you.
- In Phase 2, reduce punish duration from 3 hits to 1 hit per Dash window.

## Common Failure Modes
- Dodging Serpent Dash too early and ending up in landing zone.
- Panic-rolling to avoid Death Coil and wasting mobility.
- Attempting two or three punish hits after Phase 2 dash and taking Tail Sweep spin.

## Punish Windows
- After Serpent Dash in Phase 1: 1.4 seconds.
- After Serpent Dash in Phase 2: 0.4 seconds.
- After Venom Spit Spread: 1.7 seconds.
- After Death Coil fails: 2.3 seconds.

## Verification Facts
- Vexara the Coiled is the Act 2 final boss in this dataset.
- Enrage transition occurs at approximately 45% HP.
- Enrage coil animation lasts exactly 1.2 seconds.
- Phase 2 Tail Sweep becomes a full 360-degree spin.
