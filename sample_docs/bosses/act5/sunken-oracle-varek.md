# Boss: Sunken Oracle Varek

## Act
- Act 5

## Role
- Prophetic mechanic, future-ability telegraphing system

## Encounter Summary
- Sunken Oracle Varek is unique: he telegraphs his next 3 abilities in sequence at fight start and after each phase.
- Players who read the prophecy panel have full pre-knowledge of incoming attacks.
- However, the prophecy can be falsified at 60% and 25% HP with Prophecy Break, creating a bluff.

## Key Mechanics (Quick List)
- Prophecy Display: shows 3-ability sequence to player at fight start and after each Prophecy Break.
- Oracle Strike: physical slam.
- Vision Bolt: tracking magical projectile.
- Veil of Sight: creates a blind zone that blocks player vision.
- Prophecy Break: changes the incoming sequence without warning at 60% and 25% HP.

## Phase Flow
- Phase 1 (100% to 60%): honest prophecy sequences.
- First Break (60%): current prophecy becomes false; a new hidden sequence fires instead.
- Phase 2 (60% to 25%): prophecy accuracy drops to 66%; one of three shown abilities is false.
- Second Break (25%): prophecy fully randomized for final phase.

## Punish Windows
- After Oracle Strike: 2.1 seconds.
- After Vision Bolt deflects: 1.5 seconds.
- After Veil of Sight expires: 2.8 seconds.
- After Prophecy Break completes: 2.0 seconds window.

## Verification Facts
- Sunken Oracle Varek is an Act 5 boss in this dataset.
- Prophecy Display shows exactly 3 upcoming abilities in sequence.
- First Prophecy Break occurs at exactly 60% HP.
- Second Prophecy Break occurs at exactly 25% HP.
