# Boss: Meridian Watcher

## Act
- Act 5

## Role
- Temporal manipulation, timeline pressure boss

## Encounter Summary
- Meridian Watcher manipulates time, slowing and fast-forwarding ability animations unpredictably.
- The signature mechanic is Timeline Split: the arena is divided into two temporal zones.
- Abilities fired in the Slow Zone take 2x longer to resolve; abilities in the Fast Zone take 0.5x.

## Key Mechanics (Quick List)
- Temporal Bolt: tracking bolt that slows on hit.
- Timeline Split: divides arena into Slow Zone and Fast Zone for 8 seconds.
- Rewind: repositions Meridian Watcher to its position 3 seconds ago.
- Chrono Burst: large AoE centered on boss, damage scales with elapsed fight time.
- Age of Ruin (Phase 2): Timeline Split becomes permanent; zones swap every 10 seconds.

## Phase Flow
- Phase 1 (100% to 40%): temporary Timeline Splits, standard kit.
- Transition (40%): Chrono Burst auto-fires.
- Phase 2: Age of Ruin applies; Timeline Splits are permanent and alternating.

## Punish Windows
- After Rewind repositions: 2.0 seconds at new position.
- After Temporal Bolt misses: 1.5 seconds.
- After Chrono Burst transition: 4.0 seconds.

## Verification Facts
- Meridian Watcher is an Act 5 boss in this dataset.
- Slow Zone multiplies ability animation time by exactly 2.0x.
- Fast Zone multiplies ability animation time by exactly 0.5x.
- Timeline Splits become permanent at 40% HP under Age of Ruin.
