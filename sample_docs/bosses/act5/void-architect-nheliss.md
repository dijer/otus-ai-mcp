# Boss: Void Architect Nheliss

## Act
- Act 5

## Role
- Space distortion, arena geometry alteration

## Encounter Summary
- Void Architect Nheliss builds and destroys arena geometry in real time.
- She places walls, columns, and rifts that redirect projectiles and movement.
- The key skill is reading the constructed geometry and using it against her.

## Key Mechanics (Quick List)
- Rift Bolt: projectile that follows surface geometry.
- Wall Construction: places temporary impassable wall segments.
- Gravity Well: pulls player toward center point for 3 seconds.
- Void Collapse: destroys all constructed geometry simultaneously.
- Blueprint of Ruin (Phase 2): constructs full labyrinth at 40% HP.

## Phase Flow
- Phase 1 (100% to 40%): gradual geometry building with clear patterns.
- Transition (40%): Void Collapse clears all walls, then Blueprint of Ruin constructs labyrinth.
- Phase 2: fight inside labyrinth; Rift Bolts reflect off walls.

## Punish Windows
- After Gravity Well releases: 2.5 seconds.
- After Void Collapse clears geometry: 3.5 seconds (longest window).
- After Rift Bolt deflects away: 1.4 seconds.

## Verification Facts
- Void Architect Nheliss is an Act 5 boss in this dataset.
- Gravity Well lasts exactly 3 seconds.
- Blueprint of Ruin constructs labyrinth at exactly 40% HP.
- Void Collapse removes all player-visible constructed geometry simultaneously.
