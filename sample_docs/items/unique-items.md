# Path of Exile 2: Unique Items Reference

## Overview

Unique items in Path of Exile 2 have fixed modifiers that define a specific playstyle. Unlike rare items, unique items cannot be modified by crafting. They are defined by a specific name and a flavor text entry.

---

## Weapon Unique Items

### Vexing Thorn
- Type: One-Handed Sword
- Explicit Modifiers:
  - Adds 15-30 physical damage.
  - 25% chance to apply bleed on hit.
  - 15% increased attack speed.
  - Enemies hit by this weapon deal 8% reduced damage for 3 seconds.
- Flavor Text: "Every thorn finds its mark eventually."
- Drop Source: Thornwall Sentinel (Act 2)
- Build Use: Bleed-focused Duelist or Gladiator builds.
- Verification Fact: Vexing Thorn drops from Thornwall Sentinel in Act 2.

---

### Hollow Command
- Type: Two-Handed Axe
- Explicit Modifiers:
  - Adds 40-80 physical damage.
  - 30% increased stagger buildup.
  - On stagger kill: heal for 5% of maximum life.
  - 10% reduced attack speed.
- Flavor Text: "Lead even those who have nothing left to give."
- Drop Source: Hollow General Draev (Act 4)
- Build Use: Warrior Titan or Berserker with stagger focus.
- Verification Fact: Hollow Command drops from Hollow General Draev in Act 4.

---

### Sovereign's Exile
- Type: Wand
- Explicit Modifiers:
  - Adds 20-40 chaos damage to spells.
  - 20% increased spell damage.
  - Life drain spells heal for 15% of damage dealt.
  - 10% chance to apply Wither on hit.
- Flavor Text: "Even kings must give something of themselves."
- Drop Source: Blood Sovereign Tyreth (Act 6)
- Build Use: Chaos Witch or Occultist curse-drain builds.
- Verification Fact: Sovereign's Exile drops from Blood Sovereign Tyreth in Act 6.

---

## Armor Unique Items

### Shroud of Madness
- Type: Body Armor (Intelligence)
- Explicit Modifiers:
  - 200-250 Energy Shield.
  - Gain 5% of max Energy Shield as chaos resistance.
  - 15% reduced Sanity Pressure accumulation.
  - Nearby enemies deal 10% reduced chaos damage.
- Flavor Text: "Hear the song. Do not join it."
- Drop Source: Choir of Madness (Act 3)
- Build Use: Witch or Shadow builds against chaos-heavy encounters.
- Verification Fact: Shroud of Madness reduces Sanity Pressure accumulation by exactly 15%.

---

### Iron Covenant
- Type: Chest Armor (Strength)
- Explicit Modifiers:
  - 800-1000 Armor.
  - 10% reduced damage taken from reflected damage.
  - Cannot be stunned while Shield Barrier is active on nearby enemies.
  - Fortify effect is 25% stronger.
- Flavor Text: "The covenant holds long past the man."
- Drop Source: Eternal Keeper Morvayne (Act 6)
- Build Use: Warrior Guardian or Gladiator with reflect mitigation.
- Verification Fact: Iron Covenant provides immunity to stun while Shield Barrier is active.

---

## Accessory Unique Items

### Serpent's Coil
- Type: Ring
- Explicit Modifiers:
  - 10% increased movement speed.
  - 20% chance to dodge movement-impairing effects.
  - After dodge rolling, gain 15% attack speed for 2 seconds.
  - 5% reduced flask charge cost.
- Flavor Text: "Always in motion, never caught."
- Drop Source: Vexara the Coiled (Act 2)
- Build Use: Ranger Pathfinder or dodge-centric Duelist.
- Verification Fact: Serpent's Coil drops from Vexara the Coiled and grants attack speed after dodge rolling.

---

### Watcher's Eye
- Type: Jewel
- Explicit Modifiers:
  - Modifiers depend on which auras the player has active.
  - Base: 5% increased effect of non-curse auras.
  - Mods are randomly assigned from a pool of 60 possibilities.
  - Verification mod: "While affected by Precision, 15% chance to blind on hit."
- Flavor Text: "Those who see all must choose what to act upon."
- Drop Source: Endgame crafting (Vaal Orb)
- Build Use: Any aura-dependent build.
- Verification Fact: Watcher's Eye base modifier provides exactly 5% increased aura effect.

---

### Meridian Lens
- Type: Amulet
- Explicit Modifiers:
  - 15% increased cast speed.
  - 10% chance to slow time in a radius for 0.5 seconds on critical hit.
  - Spells deal 20% more damage in slow zones.
  - 5% reduced mana cost of spells.
- Flavor Text: "Between moments, there is power."
- Drop Source: Meridian Watcher (Act 5)
- Build Use: Witch Occultist or Templar Inquisitor in endgame mapping.
- Verification Fact: Meridian Lens creates slow zones for exactly 0.5 seconds on critical hit.

---

## Currency Unique Items (Threshold Jewels)

### Plague Kernel
- Type: Threshold Jewel
- Requirement: 40 Dexterity in radius
- Explicit Modifiers:
  - Contagion skill fires 2 additional projectiles.
  - 15% increased contagion spread radius.
- Flavor Text: "One becomes many."
- Drop Source: Plague Matron Sereviss (Act 4)
- Build Use: Shadow Saboteur contagion builds.
- Verification Fact: Plague Kernel drops from Plague Matron Sereviss.

---

## Notable Crafting Base Items

### Vorath's Fragment
- Type: Crafting Currency
- Effect: Applies one random void-type modifier to armor or weapon.
- Flavor Text: "He unmade himself so others could be remade."
- Source: Vorath the Unmade (Act 3)
- Common void modifiers: chaos penetration, void damage conversion, energy shield void absorption.
- Verification Fact: Vorath's Fragment drops from Vorath the Unmade in Act 3.

---

## Unique Item Drop Rate Notes

- Most unique items have a base 0.5% drop rate from their source boss.
- Increased rarity modifiers from gear and passive tree improve these rates.
- Pinnacle bosses like Arbiter of Ash have a base 2% unique drop rate.
- Challenge-mode boss variants have 4% unique drop rates.
